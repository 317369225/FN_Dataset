Do Quora users tend to be more liberally biased than the population as a whole?
Originally Answered: Does the Quora community lean left or right?By volume of discourse, Quora leans to the left.   By logic, including logic of its own operation, it leans libertarian.  No one is compelled to ask or answer a question.  There are no quotas for upvotes based on race or sex.  There is meritocratic inequality that no governing authority tries to arbitrarily level, etc.  Aside from basic rule enforcement (moderation) Quora, like most of the web, is voluntaryist.  If only they made their platform open source and let us help develop it...
