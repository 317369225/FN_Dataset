What are some alternatives to standard written music notation?
Color Coded Music is an alternative music notation system that  standardizes the staffs, adds color to the staffs and to the 8 piano groups.  Colored lettered stickers for the piano that correspond to the colored staffs.  It also renames black key notes, H, I, J, K, and L and gives them a diamond shape so they stand out.   Free hymn, "Be Thou My Vision" on the website: www.ColorCodedMusic.com Check out this video.
