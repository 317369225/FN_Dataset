How do you say numbers, from 0 to 10, in your language? How do you pronounce it? And what language is it?
Russian: 0 ноль, pronounced nol' 1 один, a-DIN 2 два, dva 3 три, tri 4 четыре, che-TY-re 5 пять, pyat' 6 шесть, shest' 7 семь, sem' 8 восемь, VO-sem' 9 девять, DE-vyat' 10 десять, DE-syat' The sign ' indicates the soft sign, e.g. "gel" in English is gel' in Russian.
