What are the advantages of hiring someone who has been in the US military?For a non-military job.
There are many, many advantages to hiring military veterans for civilian jobs, including (but not limited to) dedication and responsibility, the ability to learn quickly, teamwork and loyalty, skill sets that are highly cultivated, flexibility, and the capacity to execute tasks with precision. If you're looking to hire men and women who have served in the U.S. Military, check out Veterans Jobs, Veterans Careers, Civilian Careers. RecruitMilitary specializes in connecting employers and military veterans nationwide.
