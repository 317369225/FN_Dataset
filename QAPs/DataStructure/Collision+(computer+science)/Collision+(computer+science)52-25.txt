Psychology of Everyday Life: What are some of the greatest examples of presence of mind?
On 5 August 1888, without telling her husband and without permission of the authorities, Bertha Benz (Wife of Karl Benz) drove with her sons Richard and Eugen, thirteen and fifteen years old, in one of the newly constructed Patent Motorwagen automobiles—from Mannheim toPforzheim—becoming the first person to drive an automobile over a real distance Since the vehicle was not properly tested she realised the brakes were not effective as friction between two steel surface was not enough to stop a vehicle. Bertha Benz invented brake lining. She asked cobbler to  add a leather piece to the break lining which worked like a charm. Thus she can also called as inventor of brakes Benz Motorwagen
