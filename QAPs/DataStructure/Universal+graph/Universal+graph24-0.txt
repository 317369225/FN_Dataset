I have completed my BCom degree from Indian University, how do I go for charted accountancy?
Go to the nearest branch of ICAI and register yourself in Direct Entry provided your B.Com % is more than 55. After registerin with ICAI register yourself for ITT and OP complete both the programs. And register yourself under any Practicing CA for Articleship, complete 9 months of Articleship and appear for IPC exams and pass then complete remaining articleship period appear for final exams and pass and become member of ICAI (provided yu completed GMCS 1,2 nd AITT)
