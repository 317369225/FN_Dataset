What is Facebook Graph Search?
Graph Search is Facebook’s latest revision to the search feature that helps users find connections to people and places that have always existed in the graph. Facebook has a long way to go to make this search more relevant and useful to users.. Facebook is making it easier and is using it to help users discover commonalities with friends and discover places to visit...For brands, personalized search results can get your message delivered to the right people at the right time.
