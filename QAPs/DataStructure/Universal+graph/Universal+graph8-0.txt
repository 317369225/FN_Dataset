What university computer science programs excel in teaching social graph analysis or social data search?
Stanford has many classes in social graph analysis spread across multiple departments: Jure Leskovec in Computer Science teaches Social and Information Network Analysis http://www.stanford.edu/class/cs... Amin Saberi in Management Science & Engineering teaches Information Networks http://www.stanford.edu/class/ms... Jeffrey Heer and User in Computer Science and Computational and Mathematical Engineering teach Social Software http://hci.stanford.edu/courses/... Matt Jackson in Economics teaches Social and Economic Networks http://www.stanford.edu/~jackson... Ramesh Johari in Management Science & Engineering teaches Network Structures and Analysis BJ Fogg has taught several social graph related classes, e.g., a class on the Psychology of Facebook http://www.psychologyoffacebook....
