What are some of the witty remarks made by the commentators during a cricket match?
Here is one in a lighter vein with an intended pun. 1976. England vs West Indies at The Oval. England are 81 for 7. And Test Match Special commentary resumes after a break.The redoubtable Brian Johnston has this to relate – “The bowler’s Holding, the batsman’s Willey.” Quoted from Holdingwilley - Cricket News, Articles, Cartoons, Statistics
