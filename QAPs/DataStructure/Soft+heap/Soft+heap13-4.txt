What are some cool words used in your language, with their meaning?
My native language is English. In my opinion, one the coolest words is Serendipity. According to Merriam-Webster, serendipity means: Luck that takes the form of finding valuable or pleasant things that are not looked for Full Definition of SERENDIPITY :  the faculty or phenomenon of finding valuable or agreeable things not sought for; also :  an instance of this In other words, serendipity is a happy accident!
