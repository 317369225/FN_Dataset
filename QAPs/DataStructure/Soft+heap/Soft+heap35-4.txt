Alternate Histories (Hypothetical Historical Scenarios): What would happen if the US became truly imperialistic?
A better question might be, "What would happen if the US DIDN'T become truly imperialistic?"   Why do you think many American cities and states have names taken from various Native American languages?   Why do you think many of our states have Spanish names?   How do you think the United States acquired Puerto Rico?   How do you think the United States acquired Hawaii?   Google the term "manifest destiny" to learn more.
