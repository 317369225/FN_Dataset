What are some good recipes using Garbanzo beans other than Hummus?
Wheat, Tomato, Parsley, Cucumber Salad (even the kids love it) 1 cup bulgur wheat (let it soak while preparing the rest of the salad) 1 to 1 1/2 cup chickpeas a k a garbanzos (cooked & slightly smashed) 1 to 2 cups thinly sliced cucumber 1 to 1 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes (cherry tomatoes work great) 1/2 cup chopped scallions 3/4 to 1 cup minced fresh parsley Dressing 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil 3/4 cup fresh lemon juice Salt to taste Drain bulgur (pressing out water) Mix with chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, scallions and parsley.
