What books should entrepreneurs read?
I agree with John Geare - you can’t learn entrepreneurship from a book. However, that doesn’t mean that you can’t get a lot of really good knowledge to help you on your way, because someone who doesn’t read is going to make more dumb mistakes than someone who reads and pays attention. For the most part, I’d stay away from business books.
