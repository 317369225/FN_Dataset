How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?

(Or a day or whatever.) Just sit and think all about it. No doing anything else. You don't have to drink, do anything cathartic, nothing. Just sit and think about everything related to your relationship: the fights, the break up, the beginning, the fun times, etc. Think about why you broke up, why it didn't work out and so on.
