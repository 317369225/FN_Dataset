Where can I find startup advisors?
There are two types of advisors you might want: people well connected in the tech / vc world -- those will intro you to the VCs, bloggers and help with the startup side of your business; they are also (from my experience) easier to find and convince as they are used to taking the role of advisors in exchange for a small percentage of the company, people who are experts in your domain -- those will help you in your business, with the marketing, sales, intros to your customers and partners; they are harder to get as they are not so used to working with startups, often they are directors / VPs ...
