What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
The general opinion among the critics is that The Wrath of Khan was best. I'm inclined to agree. After the disappointment of Star Trek: The Motion Picture, it saved the franchise from fading into obscurity, offered great derring-do, and brought back a much-loved villain in a dramatic fashion. As for the worst, I can't stand the reboot with the younger characters at all. This may just be sentiment, but it messes with too much tradition.
