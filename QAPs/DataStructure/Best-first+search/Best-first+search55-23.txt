How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
There's no silver bullet for it. And the word 'forget' is a bit harsh. If you are talking of literally forgetting your ex, it may never happen. What can happen is that you become indifferent to him/her. You should strive to reach that stage where you can see or think or hear about them and then get on with your life like nothing happened. And this is easily achievable. All it requires is willpower. I'm my opinion, getting things out of your system is the first step. Talk to your best friend, sibling cousin, parent.
