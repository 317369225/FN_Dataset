What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
Best? Unquestionably Wrath of Khan. It actually holds up well as just a film, let alone a Star Trek film. Worst? For me it was a toss up between Search for Spock (III) and Final Frontier (V). III had a better plot, but was kind of a one-trick pony to get to IV. V had a lame plot, but had some great moments of humor. I know there are people who hate the latest movie (Into Darkness), and even the original reboot. I thought the way they rebooted was brilliant.
