Is Google Business the best first step to boost a client’s search placement?
No, build a solid website first. Creating a Google My Business local business listing has value for increasing presence in the local search results. (Google My Business - Get Your Free Business Listing on Google - Google) Google only wants you to create a listing if you have a physical location your customers do business with you. (Not your home.) You can create/claim a Bing Places listing as well. ( Bing Places for Business ) So I'd start with building a solid foundation — good, fast, mobile responsive website.
