What are the best responses for a startup who is told "Someone else is already doing X"?
Well, ideally YOU should know before hand if someone else is doing something similar to what you propose to do (or are doing). That said, in most categories there is space for a number of players to co-exist. However, if someone else is already doing what you intend to do, it is important to think through why you will have a reasonable chance to succeed in the market.
