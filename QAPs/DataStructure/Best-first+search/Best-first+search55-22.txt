How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
I was once told the "secret formula" for getting over an ex (male or female). It does not sound pleasant, but I believe it works. Call your ex and say these three things sincerely (you have to mean it or it doesn't work): "I love you." "I want you to be happy without me." "I promise to be happy without you." If you can get to a place where you can say those three things without cynicism, resentment, or self-pity, you are cured.... (more)
