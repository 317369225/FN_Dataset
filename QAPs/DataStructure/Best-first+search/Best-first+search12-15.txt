What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
I don’t know about the best, but the one I like the most is First Contact. It’s the ultimate showdown against the Federation’s greatest enemy, and they throw in some time-travel to boot. Sure they retconned the Borg a little bit (adding in the Borg queen) but it’s a fun movie with great action, tense moments, and excellent performances (for the most part). While most would undoubtedly say that Wrath of Khan is the best, First Contact just feels more epic and entertaining. You can’t go wrong with First Contact.
