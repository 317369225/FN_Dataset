What are the best responses for a startup who is told "Someone else is already doing X"?
If you get this response, you've either 1) not communicated your business differentiation effectively or 2) failed to research your competition. If you do both of these and come up with compelling reasons as to why you will succeed (or prove you already are) this should be an easy statement to counter. Also, remember how strategy plays into this, not just your product or service. 116 Views
