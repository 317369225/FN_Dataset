How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
I think the best thing to do is not to get over them. Try to see others not as possessions you can have or lose but as equals to yourself who can share and receive love and knowledge with you. Use your experience with them as a means to create something new in your life that you can share with others. Its hard but try not to waste that love and insight into the human condition by feeling like you lost something.
