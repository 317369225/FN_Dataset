What are the best travel hacks?
Everyone says this, but it's always worth saying again: Never, ever check a bag if you possibly can avoid it. You're better off doing laundry a couple times in a hostel bathroom. You might also meet interesting people at a coin-op laundry. The MEI Voyageur (http://www.meivoyageur.com/) is a great bag. Tough fabric, fits in an airplane overhead bin, and converts from duffle to backpack. Get a credit card with no international transaction or exchange fees. (CapitalOne offers one.)
