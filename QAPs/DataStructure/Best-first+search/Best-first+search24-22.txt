What are some of the best ways to start a conversation with a girl?

A genuine, polite greeting beats a cheesy pick-up line any day. In any situation, try a straightforward introduction. For example: "Hi, my name is Bob. What's your name?" In a bar, you can offer to buy the girl a drink. For example: "Hi, my name is Joe. Can I buy you a drink?" Ask her how she is doing.
