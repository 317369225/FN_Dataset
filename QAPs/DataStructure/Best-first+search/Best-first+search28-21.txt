What are some interesting startups in the education space? Why are they interesting?
Udacity, Coursera, edX, and AltSchool appear to be the edtech startups that are differentiating themselves most from their competition, and will likely have the greatest impact on the educational technology space over the next decade. Udacity is focusing heavily on technology skills, Coursera and edX on general higher education, and AltSchool on K-8 primary education. 1.
