What books should entrepreneurs read?
Here are the first two books that change my life forever: (1) Secrets of the Millionaire Mind and (2) Rich Dad Poor Dad. I read them when I was 16. And from that point onward, I set to become an entrepreneur. Of course, I didn’t start out as an entrepreneur. I first worked as a waiter. But every day for 10 years, I was thinking about how to become a successful entrepreneur and investor. Since the age of 16.
