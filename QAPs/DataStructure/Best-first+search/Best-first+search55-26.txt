How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
You don't forget; you grieve. Grieve your loss. Grieve your mistakes. Grieve their mistakes. Remember the good, recall the bad, and then let your heart do the work. Sometimes we hold onto past relationships because we have nothing else to hold onto. Sometimes we hold on because we fear if we let go, we will forget. We also hold on because of what they represented to us; it was a part of our identity. And sometimes we hold on because loneliness is just too hard to bear. So we hold on.
