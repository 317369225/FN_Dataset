What are some mind-blowing facts about Google?
There is a strong reason why the Google homepage loads the fastest as compared to any other website's (which is generally the reason why many people open it to test their connection). You need a little knowledge about TCP/IP to understand this. The RFC regarding TCP specifies that the TCP sender's window size must be 1 as a TCP connection is initiated and then it may grow exponentially. This means that the website can send just 1 packet of data in the beginning, then 2, then 4 until there is a packet loss.
