How can one be well prepared to answer data structure/algorithm questions in interviews?
The questions depend on your level of experience and position you are interviewing for. In general, companies try to assess how good you are in things you claim to know. If you are assuming a job in software, there are several types of questions Standard programming questions - operations on linked lists, tree traversals, etc. You are expected to write code on the board (or in a shared editor) and fix any mistakes you may make at first.
