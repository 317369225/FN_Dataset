What are the best travel hacks?
Here are my top tips and hacks! When you’re looking for cheap flights: Search in incognito mode: Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + N. This ways, cookies are reset and the website doesn’t raise the prices. Repeat this for every search (close all tabs and start over). Cross-reference the best flight seach engines: My preference goes to Skyscanner by far but I also make sure to check the ugly but thorough Matrix Airfare Search that looks into many flight search engines at once.
