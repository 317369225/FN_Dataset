What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
For me (and most others) the best Star Trek movie was Star Trek: The Wrath of Khan.  The film had a charismatic and menacing villain in Khan, suspenseful space battles, a terrific score, science fiction technology with moral implications, thoughtful moments about the aging process, and the emotional impact of Spock's death.
