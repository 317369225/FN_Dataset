What is the best way to determine a first target CPA prior to gathering any PPC search campaign data?
Depends on what your goal is // industry type, but you can use Unit Economics to find this pretty easily: Selling Goods: If you’re advertising a specific product for sales on your site, you can subtract your costs from selling point of the product to get your profit margin. That gives you an upper bound for target CPA - if you hit that CPA you’re breaking even on the transaction. Selling Recurring Service: If you offer a subscription service, you want to calculate the LTV (Life Time Value) of a single customer.
