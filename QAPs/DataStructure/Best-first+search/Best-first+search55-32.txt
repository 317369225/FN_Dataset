How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
Break ups are a painful and emotionally overwhelming experience and the process of moving on is different for different people. Everyone has their own time and pace to heal from a break up. Some people might take months while others might take more than a year. The pace and time would also depend on whether you both are in touch or not as being in contact and communicating can lead to maintaining of those emotions and memories.
