Where can I find startup advisors?
A really good place you can find advisors is on this platform called BEAM (iOS App / Android App). There are many many entrepreneurs, investors, professionals, and likeminded people who hang around there. It’s a free platform and you can reach out to them directly without having to “add a friend” (basically saving lots of time). You could definitely get lots of advice and support there for your business. Most successful people could also become your advisors.
