What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
(All 12 films in order of quality) BestStar Trek II: The Wrath of Khan ★★★★★ Star Trek is about the human condition and none of the films examine that more  than Star Trek II.  It is the most serious and dramatic of the films as it examines aging, loss, death, revenge, succession, and the decisions we make.  Ricardo Montalban is gripping as the revenge driven Khan - a maddened yet dangerous shadow of his former self.  The director, Nicholas Meyer steals from the best, overlaying Dickens and WWII submarine battle films.  And the ending is one of the most emotionally affecting films I've seen....
