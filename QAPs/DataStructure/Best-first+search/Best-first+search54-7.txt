What are some mind-blowing facts about Google?
In 1999, Google wanted to sell itself to Excite for $1 million. Excite CEO George Bell rejected Page and Brin’s $1 million price. Vinod Khosla, one of Excite's venture capitalists tried to negotiate the offer with Brin and Page to $750,000, which was unsuccessful and Google became a $180 billion company by 2010.
