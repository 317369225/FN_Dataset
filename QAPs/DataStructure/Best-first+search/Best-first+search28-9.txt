What are some interesting startups in the education space? Why are they interesting?
Praxis is my favorite. I could be biased, I work there, BUT, here are my reasons: Praxis is one of the few startups in the space that is truly challenging traditional educational norms. We don't create an artificial distinction between the learning and the creative process. All learning focuses around the creation of a particular product, be it a blog, a project, an actual "product," a podcast, etc. We're getting rid of traditional gate keepers like college degrees and HR departments by getting students who don't have formal credentials amazing jobs at dynamic companies.
