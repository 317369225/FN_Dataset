What's the best way to push unflattering articles off the first page of Google search results?
One of the best ways is to be proactive and start the process of building positive online reviews. Find the most relevant review sites and develop an ongoing strategy of gathering reviews from satisfied clients. Your clients will have to post the reviews so make it easy for them by guiding them through the process. If you don't have the resources, hire a firm to help you. Online reputation management should be ongoing and every business should have a plan in place. In the long run, you'll save time, money and headaches!
