What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
The best Star Trek movie is Star Trek: First Contact First Contact was a make or break movie for the Star Trek franchise - the first to rely entirely on the Next Generation cast. First Contact had a great script, the best ever villains (the Borg) and good special effects. It was a commercial success (First Contact was the best-performing Star Trek film in international markets until the reboot) and also liked by the critics (winning or being shortlisted for dozens of awards)....
