How can one be well prepared to answer data structure/algorithm questions in interviews?
Start with solving problems in Cracking the Coding Interview book. They have easy to medium level of difficulty and will cover the basic data structures (arrays, strings, lists, stacks, queues, trees and graph) and algorithms (bit manipulation, recursion and dynamic programming, sorting and searching, scalability and memory limits) that you need to know. After that, you can practice on LeetCode. Other online sites where you can practice or find interview questions: GeeksForGeeks, HackerRank, CareerCup, Glassdoor.
