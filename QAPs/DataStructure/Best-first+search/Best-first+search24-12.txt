What are some of the best ways to start a conversation with a girl?
Make and maintain eye contact. What you do next is what most people call "small talk." Introduce yourself and start talking about what you do, what you like to do, hobbies, interests, things like that. Compliment her on something or offer to buy her a drink. If a girl finds you interesting and feels that she has a connection with you she'll probably want to know more about you. It sounds like a lot of work, but it really isn't.
