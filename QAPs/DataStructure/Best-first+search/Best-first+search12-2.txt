What was the best Star Trek movie? What was the worst?
The Best: Most everyone tends to agree that Star Trek II: The Wrath of Khan is the best Star Trek film. It's very well-constructed both as a Star Trek film and as a movie in general. The characters were dealing with internal conflicts associated with growing older and having new responsibilities in their new ranks. Amidst their internal struggles, a villain attacks and forces them to make hard choices quickly.
