What are the best travel hacks?
1. Keep copies of travel docs Nothing   is worse than losing your passport or tickets and getting  stranded in   foreign land. Always keep a copy of your passport in every  piece of   baggage. An extra copy of air ticket & visa won’t hurt if  you   recycle them when you return. 2. Distribute your currency Don’t   put all your eggs in one basket; spread money across your bags and    some in your wallet.
