What are some of the best ways to start a conversation with a girl?
Figure out something you have in common with her. Then ask her about it. Ex. You know she likes chess and so do you. "Hey, so do you play chess? Oh, you do. Cool. So do I. So what's your playing style....." If you know nothing about her and this is some random encounter, then keep this in mind: you lose nothing if you don't talk to her. It's fine not to. That said, if you're in some social situation or grouped around a particular activity (like say you're at a roller-skating rink), then talk to her about that activity.
