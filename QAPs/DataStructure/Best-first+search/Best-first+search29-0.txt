What is the best way to determine a first target CPA prior to gathering any PPC search campaign data?
Hi there! This is Colette from Step One Growth. Before you can know your target, first you need to know how much you’re going to make per customer. If the product you’re selling is $10, you need to acquire customers for less than $10 in order to make money. For one-time purchases, the general rule of thumb is 3:1 for price per product to CPA target. For recurring monthly/yearly purchases, it’s flipped 1:3 for price per month/year to CPA target.
