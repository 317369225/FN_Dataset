What books should entrepreneurs read?
Without a doubt, the following three, in no particular order. Just go buy them now -- especially the first two -- and read them immediately: UPDATE: Entrepreneur America, by Rob Ryan of Ascend Communications. Yes it was written in 2000, but its Tim Ferris-esque "4-hour-workweek" step-by-step walkthrough lessons of actually building your startup, preparing and pitching to investors, and managing the money you receive and building your company are simply awesome. Complete with self-assessments and exercises, it's a really great primer on all things the new entrepreneur needs to know and do. ... (more)
