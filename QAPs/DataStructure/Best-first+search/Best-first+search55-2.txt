How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
When you have truly loved someone, you will never really "get over him", or her. You love (d) them. They were a significant part of you, and that will remain forever - and it should. What we want to "get over" is the sort of obsessive thinking and distress that we feel when a relationship leaves and we cannot or should not get it back again. That is primarily a matter of two things, I believe - focus and distance - and this is where the remedy lies.
