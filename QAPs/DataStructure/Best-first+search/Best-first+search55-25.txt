How do I forget my ex after everything we've been through?
By choosing to move on. The past relationship is over, just like the Peloponnesian War - so there is nothing holding you to it. The hold is in your mind, and you must release it in order to move forward. You may have had some good times, and some memories - and those will always stay with you. The person, however, won't stay with you. You may be missing the memories but not the person. There's nothing that requires you to tarnish the cherished moments, instead you can keep them for as long as you want, yet your future is not in the past, it's ahead of you. ... (more)
