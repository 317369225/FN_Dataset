What are the best responses for a startup who is told "Someone else is already doing X"?
There isn't, and there shouldn't be, an automatic way to answer to this question. The best answer is one that demonstrates a clear understanding of the difference and your competitive edge. And that answer depends on you, the other company and the person who said that someone else is already doing X. If you've never heard of that other company, someone caught you unprepared. Oops. I suggest to follow Axel Schultze's advice and say something like "I haven't heard of them, but I'll definitely check them out". Then go home, do your homework and make sure it doesn't happen again.
