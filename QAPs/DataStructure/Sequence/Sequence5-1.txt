What was the purpose of the surveillance drone sequence in Interstellar?
It was an early chase scene to add some excitement to the first act of the movie. It also brought in some exposition about the background of their world (there was worldwide war 10 years ago and those tools are now abandoned. And, as Minhaz says, the entire scene allowed character development of Coop and Murph. They share an interest, they are bound. Demonstrating such relationships early is critical if we are going to make the core of the movie about them dealing with their separation.
