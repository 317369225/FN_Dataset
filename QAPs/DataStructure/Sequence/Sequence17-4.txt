What are the best Bollywood dance sequences?
As others have mentioned, Dil Se's moving train sequence is usually taught as the reference Bollywood dance sequence in film school. It's definitely a very difficult feat to pull off and it's done exquisitely well. The shots are amazing. The dance sequence for Udi Baba from Vidhaata (1982) is a favorite of mine. It's fun, trippy and has great set design. And the song is really catchy. Udi Baba - Vidhaata (1982)
