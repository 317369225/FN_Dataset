Are startups overvalued right now?
Startups are overvalued right now, in aggregate, although valuations are reportedly coming down.  Downward trends are not showing in the numbers yet.  At every level, valuations are about double the historical median and more for the last rounds raised (Series D+)  likely fueled by the Unicorn valuations.
