What is the best way to explain "depth-first search" to a non-CS student?
Imagine you are in a city A and aim to visit all the cities in your state. To start the journey, from city A, you will visit any random unvisited neighbouring city. From that city, you can again visit any unvisited neighbouring city. You can keep doing this until you are in a situation that there are no more unvisited neighbour city. In this case, you are allowed to come back to the previous city. You can keep coming back till you find an unvisited neighbouring city.
