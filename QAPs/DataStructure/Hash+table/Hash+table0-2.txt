How do hash tables work? How do distributed hash tables work?
I'll answer for a specific use - password protection. When you log into a site like Quora, you have to enter a password. If you forget your password, it's no good calling Quora and asking them to remind you, because they don't know it. Huh? When you log in, your password is fed into a hashing algorithm that turns your "HELLO123" password into something like "f51cd8963". This is the "password" that's stored against your user name.
