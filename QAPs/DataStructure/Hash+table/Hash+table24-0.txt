Which is the most popular routing technique for distributed hash tables?
Depending on what you mean by "popularity," there are two different answers. If you mean popular among academics, as measured by number of published papers, it appears that Chord/Pastry -style routing wins, with a combined 1829 entries appearing in citeseer for "chord dht" and "pastry dht." Kademlia, in contrast, only gets 215 hits for "kademlia dht." If you mean popular among implementors, there is no contest. Kademlia comes out on top.
