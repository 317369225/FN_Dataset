What is the difference between HashSet, HashMap and hash table? How do they behave in a multi-threaded environment?
A HashSet is an unordered set of unique values. A HashMap is (basically) an unordered map of keys to values. A Hashtable is a synchronized (only one thread can access it at a time) HashMap.
