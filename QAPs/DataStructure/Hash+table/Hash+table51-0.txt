What is a hash table used for in computing?
Major reason? Very fast lookup in a list. You get 3 (main) methods to search through a list of data, following (very simplified explanation) listed in increasing order of speed: Linear search - think of it as a jumbled heap of pages on your desk. You need to find one with a specific heading. So you start at the 1st and look at each until you find the one with that heading. Binary search - as if those pages were sorted alphabetically (much like a telephone directory). So you can jump around narrowing down to get closer at each step to the page you're after.
