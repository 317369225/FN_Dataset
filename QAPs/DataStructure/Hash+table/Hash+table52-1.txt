What do you mean by Hash Map and Hash Table?
While the definitions can vary a bit. For the most part, a Hash Map uses hashing to give you an average look-up time of O(1) when you are storing an item under a key. It’s a lot like a dictionary, where you have a word and you look up the definition. With a Hash Map, you have a key-value, and it lets you quickly retrieve (or look-up) the item that key is representing. It lets you map item ‘X’ to item ‘Y’.
