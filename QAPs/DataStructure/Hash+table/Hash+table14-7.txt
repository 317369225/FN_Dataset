Why do hash tables need to store the key of a record?
This is actually a very good question. It took me some time to even understand it. My take is as follows: You are right. If the app stores the required keys then I can't think of a reason why the table needs to store the keys. However this is done mostly for reasons of convenience. The list of keys being available at any given time allows programmers to Iterate or display content or sort or manipulate it in any way needed.
