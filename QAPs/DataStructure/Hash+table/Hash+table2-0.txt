How do Facebook, Quora and other companies handle hash table collisions?
I can’t speak for Facebook or Quora, but I’m quite sure most companies don’t handle hash table collisions, simply because most corporate developers don’t implement their own hash tables. Instead, they use whatever associative map features are available in their language of choice; most languages in production offer such data structures with reasonable performance.
