How did famous companies get their names?
FILA The abbreviation or full form of FILA is not Finally I left Adidas as written elsewhere. The name FILA originates from FILA brothers who started the company. Abbreviation or Full Form of FILA Fila is one of the world’s largest sportswear manufacturing companies which was founded in 1911 in Italy. FILA was originally started with the intention of making clothing for the people of Italy and it later started to manufacture sportswear in the year 1970 for supporting the tennis player Björn Borg. From then onwards FILA has established itself as a brand for making sportswear for athletes all over the world and it is associated with champions from a variety of sports such as tennis, skiing, basketball, motor racing, golf, football and running.
