HackerRank has quite a few Machine Learning challenges. They also support 20 different languages including Python. There is an entire track on HackerRank dedicated to 'Machine Learning'. They also organize Machine Learning contests frequently. Quora CodeSprints are one of those contests. The training data for the challenges (Quora Answer Classifier) is sampled from the real production data of Quora. Quora is also doing an exclusive ML contest in association with HackerRank - Quora ML CodeSprint 2013
