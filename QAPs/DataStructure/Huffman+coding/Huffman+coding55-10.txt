What do you think if a senior software developer can't write code?
Senior developer isn't actually a senior job title except in very small companies. It's usually his grade 3 out of 10. If someone in that position can't code, that's a real problem. But if someone truly has 10 or 15 years of experience you would generally not expect them to write very much code. Anyone at that level will have far more value through their influence on others tha through what they could ever do themselves. But there's a difference between not being able to code and not coding.
