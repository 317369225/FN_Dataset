What do you think if a senior software developer can't write code?

My job was to help him in a general way (mainly problem solving) but every time I asked a question he grinned a little; it was obvious he thought I was an a “senior engineer who couldn’t write code”. What this engineer didn’t realize was that I had been an engineer on the Visual Studio team at Microsoft. I had literally helped write parts of the tools he used every day to write his code.
