What do you think if a senior software developer can't write code?
This can happen when someone has been in Management or Leadership roles long enough that their skills are out of date or not the same as their organization currently uses. It can also happen that a company's official titles are wrong, or that they're tied to a certain pay grade. You can still understand the fundamentals of Software Development, best practices, etc. if you don't code every day; often Software Architects and very Senior Developers find themselves in roles in which they no longer code every day.
