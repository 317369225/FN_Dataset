What do you think if a senior software developer can't write code?
I hear “x writes bad code” far too often. More often than not, it’s a sign that the person making the statement doesn’t understand that: Code is often repurposed or evolved to do something it wasn’t initially designed to do, hence it looks like a poor design for the new purpose. There are many ways to solve a problem, so someone else’s solution may look “wrong” but be perfectly sufficient. People have different priorities or perspectives based on their experience.
