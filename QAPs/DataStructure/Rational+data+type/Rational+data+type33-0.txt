Why are ISIS militants driving Texas-made Toyota trucks apparently modified for U.S. Special Forces?
At one point, someone from Toyota was actually asked the same question about Al-Qaeda by a New York Times reporter. Spokesperson Wade Hoyt offered up the following response, which is truly one of the greatest responses by any public relations professional, anywhere: ''It is not our proudest product placement,'' he said. ''But it shows that the Taliban are looking for the same qualities as any truck buyer: durability and reliability.'' AUTOS ON FRIDAY/International; Trucks of the Taliban: Durable, Not Discreet
