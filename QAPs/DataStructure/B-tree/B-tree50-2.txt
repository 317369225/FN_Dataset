What is the difference between a B-tree index and a Bitmap index in Oracle?
Internally, a bitmap and a btree indexes are very different, but functionally they are identical in that they serve to assist Oracle in retrieving rows faster than a full-table scan. The basic differences between b-tree and bitmap indexes include: 1: Syntax differences: The bitmap index includes the "bitmap" keyword.
