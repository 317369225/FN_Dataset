What's the best book for learning B and B+ tree?
Haven't read many books regarding these but the path I followed in college: B+Trees from Database System Concepts by Silberschatz, Korth and Sudarshan (http://www.db-book.com/). It covers the tree structure with query, insertion and deletion operations. It then explores the indexing usage of B+trees in databases and filesystems and how these perform when compared with other indexing techniques. It also covers B Trees in brief and explains why B+trees are preferred over it. A B+Tree visualization with insertion, deletion and queries is available at https://www.cs.usfca.edu/~galles... ...
