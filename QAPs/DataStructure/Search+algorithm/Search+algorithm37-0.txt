How do reviews factor into google's local search algorithm?
I would argue that they are the most important factor in Google’s local search algorithm. This includes results in the regular results and on Google Maps. We have analyzed 1,000’s of reviews and results for searches with local intent. Our synopsis in a nutshell is that if you do not have at least an average of a 4 star rating you will not rank on the first page of Google Map’s results and subsequently have no chance of ranking on the first page of normal results.
