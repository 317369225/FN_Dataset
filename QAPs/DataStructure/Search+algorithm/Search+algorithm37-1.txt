How do reviews factor into google's local search algorithm?
The exact formula for how reviews weigh into Google’s local search algorithm is and will probably always be a well-kept secret. But there is some research on how average rating, number of reviews, and content impact search results. Per Lowell’s answer, in the updated study done by David Mihm about local search ranking factors (Local Search Ranking Factors), Quantity of Native Google Places Reviews came in at #7 on the list of most important factors.
