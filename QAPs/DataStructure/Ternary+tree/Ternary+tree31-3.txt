Do big tech companies give a chance to freshers (new grad or entry level) who have failed some subjects and hence have taken an extra semester or year to complete their course/degree?
IBM India has a strict* policy when it comes to screening candidates: You should have scored at least 70% cumulatively till date in your college On the date of joining you should not have any backlogs. *This is based on my experience when I forward resumes to HR. There may be exceptions.
