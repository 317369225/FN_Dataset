How do I prepare for IIIT-H lateral entry?
The interview can be narrowed down to 3-4 main focus points: Favorite Topics and Interests Favorite Algorithm Last Exam Clarity Motivation for research Focus on Data Structures, Discrete Mathematics, Computer Organization and Digital Logic. Be well versed with whatever you answer as favorite topics and interests as they tend to ask questions relating to that only. Be confident and clear with your answers as they might try to trick you with alternate solutions. Spend time trying to get your fundamentals clear, concept clarity is the theme.
