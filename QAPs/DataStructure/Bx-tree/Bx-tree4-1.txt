Can you name some small, helpful "lifehacks" that soldiers use?
Insulating tape is really useful. If you're out hiking or travelling, a roll of insulating tape never hurts. packing a bag- use Large ziplocks and organise your stuff- all Tops in one, all bottoms in another, one more for underwear, a smaller one for toiletries and one more for miscellaneous gear. Roll them up to save space. makes finding things dead easy. Carabiners- clip everything you need, especially important things, to yourself. they will never get lost. reading a map and compass. tying knots Camping grade ziploc bags are great for keeping smartphones dry and clean, and can still be sued without taking them out. keeps paper dry, too. Bungee cords are great for improvised clothelines, and just about 1000 other uses.
