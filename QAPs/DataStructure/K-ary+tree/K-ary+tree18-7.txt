Why is e-commerce such a hot area in venture capital now?
Many E-commerce sites show predictability about the business model early on. If you can calculate lifetime value and customer acquisition cost, and you know that one is larger than the other, then you can invest in more marketing with confidence. But that marketing takes capital, and that is where us VCs come in. In many social media sites, as the cost of building the sites has come down, there has been less call for large rounds of VC.
