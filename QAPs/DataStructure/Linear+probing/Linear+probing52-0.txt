What did Marshall McLuhan mean by hot and cool media?
Hot medium: extends single sense in high definition (HD) l   HD=well filled in with data, sharp, detailed l   Little audience participation required l     E.g. print, books, radio, photographs, movies, lecture l    Cool medium: low definition (LD) l   Offers little information l   Requires recipient to fill in missing information l   E.g. telephone, speech, cartoons, TV, seminar l   High participation
