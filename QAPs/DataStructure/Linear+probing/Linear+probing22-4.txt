What are some examples of non-linear novels?
The Time Traveller's Wife by Audrey Niffenegger. Although it  happening linearly for protagonist and therefore the story, but for the other characters we're jumping about all over the place. It certainly does have the quality of a non-linear novel, of you having to piece the bits altogether and work out yourself what happens when and more importantly, in this case, who knows what when.
