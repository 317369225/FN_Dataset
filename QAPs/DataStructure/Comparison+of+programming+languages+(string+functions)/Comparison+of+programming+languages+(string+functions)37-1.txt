Mobile Applications: What's the most innovative app?(NB: mostly for Android here)
Our emotions influence every aspect of our lives — how we learn, how we communicate, how we make decisions. Yet they’re absent from our digital lives; the devices and apps we interact with have no way of knowing how we feel. Scientist Rana el Kaliouby aims to change that. She demonstrates a powerful new technology that reads your facial expressions and matches them to corresponding emotions. This “emotion engine” has big implications, she says, and could change not just how we interact with machines — but with each other. This app knows how you feel -- from the look on your face This lady hails from Egypt and has taken technology to a whole new level by adding the parameter to measure the emotional quotient of the person. She has dedicated 15 years of her life to map the same. The facial expressions are quantified in terms of "Action unit" and the program here has an algorithm to quantify these units give the resukts in terms of Smile Brow raise Brow furrow Frown Valence EngagementBeautiful concept ..isn't it?
