If I was interviewing for a SWE position in Python, and you were the interviewer, what are the top 5 things about the language that would disappoint you if I did not know them?
How to write decorators, and when decorators should be used. Why we have "identifiers" rather than "variables" in Python, or, if you're not familiar with those terms specifically, at least understanding what implications they have for your code. Being able to write list comprehensions and generator expressions. Being unwilling to obfuscate nested loops behind list comprehensions and generator expressions. Bytes vs Unicode.
