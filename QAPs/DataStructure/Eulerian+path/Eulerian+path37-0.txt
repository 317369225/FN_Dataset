How do I become a successful entrepreneur in a short span of time?
If you're asking yourself that question, you're already in trouble. Although there are some (relatively) quick successes, they are few and far between. It's very important that you embrace the process of the startup. Not just the end goal. The end goal focus simply won't sustain you when you go through the hard stuff. Disappointment will be your undoing. Read the writings of Admiral James Stockdale.
