Has Google Reader seen a decline in usage as Twitter's popularity has grown?Brian Shih mentions "RSS" as being alien to mainstream. Twitter abstracts away RSS and provides similar functionality. Although not all users use Twitter this way, news consumption is a common use case. This is a follow-up question to Is Google Reader a popular product? Why?.
According to Comscore, monthly unique visitors to Google Reader from the US peaked in November 2009 at 1.8 million visitors. The reach hovered around 900K throughout most of 2010 before increasing to 1.4 million in November 2010 (most recent month). For comparison, My Yahoo! reached 14.8 million unique visitors in November 2010. I don't have access to worldwide stats.
