Why is e-commerce such a hot area in venture capital now?
Online shoppers are now increasing day by day and because of it the eCommerce stores are getting huge existence of people at a greater extent. Due to this the sales of the eCommerce stores have increased a lot and this is the main reason why eCommerce is such a hot area in venture capital right now in the eCommerce market. The eCommerce software has given a new shape to the eCommerce stores for gaining the huge attention of people in their website.
