Was mathematics invented or discovered?
Originally Answered: Is math invented or discovered?The language of mathematics is invented, but the objects of its study are discovered. Wikipedia's definition on Mathematics: Mathematics is the abstract study of quantity, structure, space, change, and many other topics. It has no generally accepted definition. Oxford Dictionary's definition on Mathematics: the abstract science of number, quantity, and space. Quantity, structure, space, and change ... all these "phenomena" exist in our universe and manifest in different ways. I would argue that mathematics is the study of describing these phenomena.
