God: Who gave God his name?
The English word for deity, 'God', is of Old High German origin, with no documentation from the period telling where it came from. But it looks to me to be simply an address to deity derived from the first three basic sounds that most newborn babies make, in order: 1. First intake of air, which opens the throat and thereby makes a 'g' sound; 2. The first vocalization, of more or less 'aaaaaa'; 3.
