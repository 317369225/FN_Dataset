God: Who gave God his name?
God is a word that is of the English language. And the English language is derived from the West Germanic of language, which was a part of the Proto-Germanic language. The earliest written usage of the word God, in a Germanic language, is in the Wulfila Bible or Gothic Bible from the 4th century, and it is written as "guda" and "guþ" and these words derive from the proto Germanic word for God "*ǥuđán " The origins of the word "*ǥuđán " are hypothesized to come from the Proto-Indo-European word  "*ǵʰau̯-" meaning to call or invoke.
