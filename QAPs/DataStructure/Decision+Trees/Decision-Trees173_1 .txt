What is your favorite decision tree algorithm?Like Jack Rae mentions, the C4.5 algorithm is a good algorithm. But after having worked with C5.0: An Informal Tutorial I would have to say that is my new favorite. The biggest advantage is its training time over C4.5 - its orders of magnitude faster. For big datasets this matters a lot. C5.0 was also developed by Ross Quinlan - the creator of C4.5.
