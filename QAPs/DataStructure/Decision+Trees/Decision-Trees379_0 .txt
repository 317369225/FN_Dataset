Is it possible to get decision tree classification (supervised MLAs) results with some percentage of confidence?Marginals is concept which can be used to get results of MLAs with some percentage. Suppose you have 2 class problem and you apply certain supervised MLAs technique . Marginals will give you the difference between the probability of a records been assigned to Class1 and Class2 respectively. If the difference between probaility of particular records being assigned to class1 and class2 is high. You can use this difference as certains measure of confidence.
