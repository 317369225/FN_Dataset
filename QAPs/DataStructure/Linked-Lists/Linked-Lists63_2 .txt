Why do we use a circular linked list when we can achieve the same implementation just by starting from the head again when we reach the NULL node?You are right in saying that you can start again from the head when you encounter NULL. But you have to incorporate extra logic in your program to check whether its NULL and if yes, set it to the head node. In case of circular linked list, we have to bother with no such thing. Some of the applications of the circular lists are: Circular buffers for applications like video and audio players.
