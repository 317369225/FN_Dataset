what do `` non-pathological datum '' mean ? pathological datum be datum that will make the algorithm perform bad . for hash table , pathological datum be datum that cause collision . that of course depend on the hash function be use . for example , if you hash function add the character together : <code> hash -lrb- `` abcd '' -rrb- = a + b + c + d </code> . then pathological datum look like : <code> -lcb- `` abcd '' , `` dcba '' , `` cbda '' , ... -rcb- </code> . any permutation of <code> `` abcd '' </code> will hash to the same position so you will end up with a link list which you be try to avoid in the first place . non-pathological datum be datum that be not pathological .
