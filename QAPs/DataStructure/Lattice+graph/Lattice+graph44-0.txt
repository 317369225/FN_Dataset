What are some seriously insightful or enlightening infographics?
Charles Minard's classic depiction of the Russian campaign of Napoleon's Army. It contains four axis (geographic x/y, size of the army, temperature during the retreat), all in one compelling and easily understood graphic. It tells the story of a horrible human tragedy with details that nowadays are forgotten, but back then were very present to the audience of this graphic (especially the crossing of the Berezina River on their way back, which cost many lives).
