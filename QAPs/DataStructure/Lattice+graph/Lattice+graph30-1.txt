A taxonomy is structured like a tree or hierarchy; what is an example of a classification system that is structured like a lattice?
The way some auto makers, like BMW and Mercedes, name their models: with a letter or number for the chassis and a number for the engine. In fact, this is the way most modular industrial products are classified, see f.e. the names for rolling bearings at SKF: http://www.skf.com/portal/skf/ho...
