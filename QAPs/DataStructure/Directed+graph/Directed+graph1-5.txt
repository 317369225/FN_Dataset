How do I detect a cycle in a directed graph?
In layman's terms This is no different than the case of: You started to reach somewhere, but you reached an intermediate point that you have already reached before. To know if you re-reached an intermediate point, all you have to do is mark it, say if you reached an area & marked it with a chalk or on to a stone. When travelling further to reach destination, assume you re-reached an area that is marked earlier, you know you have been/detected a cycle. You can use any of the tree traversals.
