What are some of the best "Shers" (please provide English translation too)?
YE  KHAMOSH  MIJAAJ  JINDAGI  JEENE  NAHI  DEGI IS  DOUR  ME  YADI  JEENA  HAI,  TO  KOHRAAM  MACHA  DALO These lines were said by Govind Jaiswal during his interview, who secured 48th rank in Civil service examination. His father, a rickshaw vendor, toiled hard, sold off land so that the son can get education. Complementing his father's struggle and dream, Govind ranked 48th in his very first attempt in Civil Service Examination, 2006 – among 474 candidates.
