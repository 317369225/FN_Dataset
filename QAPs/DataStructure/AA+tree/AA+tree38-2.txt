What jargon and abbreviations should newcomer DOTA2 players familiarize themselves with?
If playing with people in India, BC, MC, MKC, TMKC, Laude ke baal (LKB. Not to be confused with MKB/BKB). These will be explained by the people using them, to you. These are situational terms and may be used as an exclamation or direct reference based on the current scenario/context. Your best bet would be to ask the person in what context he meant it. GG (Good Game)
