What are some of the best "Shers" (please provide English translation too)?
Aye chaand, tu kis mazhab ka hai, Eid bhi teri, aur Karwa chauth bhi. Translation: Dear moon you belong to which religion. Eid is yours and so is  Karwa chauth.   Eid is an important religious holiday celebrated by Muslims worldwide that marks the end of Ramadan, the Islamic holy month of fasting.    Karwa chauth is a one-day festival celebrated by Hindu women in North India in which married women fast from sunrise to moonrise for the safety and longevity of their husband.
