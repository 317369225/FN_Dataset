What's the best sentence ever formed?
"I never said she stole my money." This sentence has seven different meanings depending on which word is stressed. It brings out the importance of prosody in language. Prosody is the rhythm, stress, and intonation of speech. It reflects various features of the speaker or the utterance: the emotional state of the speaker; the form of the utterance (statement, question, or command); the presence of irony or sarcasm; emphasis, contrast, and focus; or other elements of language that may not be encoded by grammar or choice of vocabulary.
