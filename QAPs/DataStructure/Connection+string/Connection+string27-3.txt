What are the challenges faced by CS/IT engineers when hired by mass recruiters like TCS, Infosys, Wipro?
challenges are 1. how to move up the organisational hierarchy if decided to stay with company for long time. 2. otherwise finding reasons to quit and have a go at dream job. 3. getting mastery over the domain you have been assigned (some times you get to choose your domain). 4. getting appraisals companies think engineers as commodities so as they can trade our skills for companies profit. companies don't take an account of your aspirations and goals.
