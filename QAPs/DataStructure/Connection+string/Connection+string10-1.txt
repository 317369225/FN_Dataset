Epics and folklore of India: Is there any connection between String Theory and the Sanskrit sound of Hindu origin "Aum"?
It turns out, despite all expectations to the contrary, string theorists actually have a sense of humour. So, yes OM stands for open membranes in string theory, and as a (long due) tribute to all the pseudoscientific psychobabble that abounds in our mysticism-obsessed world, it is sometimes rendered as ॐ.
