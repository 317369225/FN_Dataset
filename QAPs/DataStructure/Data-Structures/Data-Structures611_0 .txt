How do HFT's manage queue priority?In a low latency context, you aim to process each event ASAP. This usually means processing it before another one arrives. As such priorities are not meaningful when you aim to have at most one outstanding event. You rarely have priority messages, so you can have a queue for your main messages and another queue for the priority ones.
