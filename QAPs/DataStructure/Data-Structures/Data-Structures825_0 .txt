What are the must know data structures and algorithms to crack the Google and Facebook interview?For a semi-exhaustive list, take a look at the great book “Cracking the coding Interview”. It gives examples of the questions you might be asked, as well as what you should know about algorithms (sorting, tree search etc.) and data structures (linked lists, hash maps etc.). The list is not that long but the book gives detailed explanations of the advantages and drawbacks of each, and why you should know them.
