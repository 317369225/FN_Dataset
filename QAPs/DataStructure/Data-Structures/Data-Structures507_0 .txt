Is plinth a structural element of building?Perhaps you mean to ask if plinth is a load bearing structural element. Plinth in itself is more of a locational term unless you mean a plinth beam. Plinth beam is a load bearing element. Plinth may not always transfer load. Plinth is defined as the raised level or platform at ground floor level OR support base of a structure or building.
