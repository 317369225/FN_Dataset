Does the Universe have an underlying Data Structure?Within a restricted (computer-science-type) meaning of data structure: The Universe does not have an underlying data structure. Like a natural spoken language, a data structure is a way of expression, created by human beings. Philosophically: The Universe may be treated as an organic, living, and motherly entity. Just as mothers have their own way of talking to their toddlers and do not need a data structure to talk, the Universe has its own way of talking to us and does not need a data structure to talk. Just as toddlers do not understand their mothers’ talks, we do not understand the Universe’s talks.
