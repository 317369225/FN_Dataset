Do PM interviews involve developer-type interview questions (i.e. data structures and algorithms) if the interviewee does not have a CS degree?Development interviews have development questions; product management interviews should have product management questions. Look at the popular activities and artifacts for defining and delivering products. Expect questions around these. Consider: How would you define our market segments? What are some personas for our products? What is the best product marketing tool for sales people?
