Why is the time complexity of Insertion and Deletion in Singly Linked List Big O(1)?Insertion and deletion by itself is a O(1) operation, at which index you are trying to insert or delete makes the difference. If you want to delete or insert an item towards the end of the list, getting to that node will take you O(n) time and if you are inserting or deleting a node at the head it takes O(1) time. 104 Views  View Upvotes
