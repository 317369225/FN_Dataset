What is the difference between lists and arrays in Python?Usually, when people say “array” when talking about Python, they mean “list”. This is because lists are fundamental to the language the way arrays are fundamental to most low-level languages. However, there is indeed a module called array in standard library, providing you with a (maybe) more efficient way to store some kinds of lists.
