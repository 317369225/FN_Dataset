I would say pick your battles first rather than fighting everywhere. You listed so many things and I think it is just looking at some job descriptions. May be you get initial idea of how these items fit in different use case and into a bigger picture. Then take a step back and think which path you want to travel first. While going any one path, if you keep an eye on the abstraction of concepts (NOT abstraction of implementations), you will fell it is very simple to learn others as well :) Posts on Let's Program may be able to help.
