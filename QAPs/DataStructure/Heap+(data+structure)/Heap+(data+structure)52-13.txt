How are students in Indian colleges taught programming?
In Chennai Mathematical Institute, Haskell is taught in the first programming course for BSc Math + CS/Physics 1st year and MSc CS 1st year students. Haskell is a beautiful and elegant language, and the course is fun and interesting, taught by very competent faculty. A huge contrast to some of the other answers. As for the specific points asked : the blackboard isn't used very much, though might be used occasionally.
