How are students in Indian colleges taught programming?
Most successful software gurus from India are self learners. Most colleges in India lack competent faculty for CS subjects. Thus, students with an inclination to learn programming or any subject for that matter do so on their own. These self learners become successful in the long run. Unfortunately, very few students (maybe less than 20% of them) are self learners. Vast majority of students use their brain merely as memory devices.
