How are students in Indian colleges taught programming?
Yes, in most of the average Indian Engineering colleges writing entire programs on the blackboard is a norm. And yes, students do copy them down and are generally expected to reproduce it exactly in their answer sheets during exams. The reason for this, in my opinion, are teachers (most, if not all) who have never come across (or shied away from) opportunities requiring them to code a live project, the rugged examination oriented education system that judges students on the basis of what they can reproduce in paper instead of what they have learnt / what they know.
