How can one be well prepared to answer data structure/algorithm questions in interviews?
I interview many people day to day. I have also gotten offers from some very difficult companies. I would recommend to read Cracking the Coding Interview: 150 Programming Questions and Solutions: Gayle Laakmann McDowell: 9780984782802: Amazon.com: Books I mean really really read it. Sit down with a text editor and write code. Do it without internet. Then read the answers and try to answer the questions again without help. Gayle Laakmann McDowell is also on Quora.
