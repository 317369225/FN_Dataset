TopCoder Cookbook - Mostly check threads with prefix algorithm competitions. Tutorials are called recipes here. Wide variety of recipes on topics from debugging tricks, memory saving tweaks etc. to possible solutions of NP complete problems can be found here. PEGWiki - Just look at the index.
