What are some tips for self-studying Data Structures and Algorithms?
Don’t aim for an exhaustive study of every single data structure and Big-O values. It will be an impressive feat, but such knowledge is seldom required in one’s career. Get comfortable with Algorithmic Complexity. Please do not ignore it. You cannot master data structures without mastering algorithmic complexity. Learn to visualize the data structure. You can draw it, see it in your head, or whatever. Just make sure that you understand the internal structure and how various operations work on them. Learn when to use a data structure.
