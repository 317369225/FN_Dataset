How can one be well prepared to answer data structure/algorithm questions in interviews?
IQ of 140 is rare. The type of questions you are looking at can be done with an IQ of 109 by practicing about 100-200 odd questions asked in interviews. After that, most of the questions become repetitions of these 100-200 questions. No one will ask you to implement a very complex problem because in 99% cases, the interviewers are also normal people with similar IQ. If you do 10 questions a day and practice the questions again after you have done all, then I would look at a time frame of about a month to be fully prepared for any interview. ... (more)
