Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology: Does the interviewer expect us to implement heaps and other data structures in the interviews?
The goal of a technical interview is usually to judge your problem solving skills and coding abilities than judge your implementation skills of standard stuff. Hence usually you will not be asked a direct question to implement a heap, rather there might be a problem which needs heap as a data structure. The interviewer doesn't have any restrictions barring few company policies hence (s)he might still choose to ask you specific implementation details if (s)he feels it is a must know thing and there is no way a potential recruit cannot know it.
