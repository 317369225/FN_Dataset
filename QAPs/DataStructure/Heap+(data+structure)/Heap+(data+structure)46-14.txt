How can one be well prepared to answer data structure/algorithm questions in interviews?
I would suggest to start doing the exercises from Cracking the Code interview. The problems have a medium level and are pretty much alike with those given in the actual interview. A great site to practice is LeetCode. They have a lot of problem sets and also if you pay a subscription you can have access to the interview questions from a particular company. Also, something that really helped me a lot, was doing mock interviews. It is really important to receive feedback and someone to teach you how to behave and think in an interview.
