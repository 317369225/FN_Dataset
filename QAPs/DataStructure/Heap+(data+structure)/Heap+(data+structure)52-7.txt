How are students in Indian colleges taught programming?

Tier - 3 ###### As many of the answers point out, most of the courses were taught by teachers who themselves had very little practical experience of handling things. Many students used to just memorize the programs. Programming in C meant TC++3.0 and in java meant notepad.
