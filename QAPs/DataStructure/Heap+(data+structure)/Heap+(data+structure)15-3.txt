What are the most important data structures in a tech interview?
It is my concept that you are never going to find questions that involved very advanced algorithms and data structures in onsite interviews specially as a fresher. Its more important to have a clear understanding of the fundamentals. Clear understanding of hash map, linked list, binary search trees, STL should get you a long way. Some other probable but little advanced topics like Trie, Heap, Disjoint set should be more than enough.
