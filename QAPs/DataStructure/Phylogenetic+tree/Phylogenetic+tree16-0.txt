Is there a way to make phylogenetic trees more readable?
Theoretically, it shouldn't be hard to output a tree in a format where the labels (species names) are in HTML, or editable by the user. There's no script I know of that does it, and I've never seen it done, but it's possible. Same for labelling branches and nodes. The problem is what you will replace the scientific names with. Vertebrates all have common names. Butterflies and most odonates have them as well. But outside of that, only the charismatic species and genera have common names.
