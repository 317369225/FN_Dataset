What would an intelligent design phylogenetic tree look like?
Just so we’re clear on my biases: I have a biology degree and am an atheist. Phylogenetic trees are diagrams that depicts the lines of evolutionary descent of different species, organisms, or genes from a common ancestor. So, it depends on the proponent of intelligent design’s conception of intelligent design. If they’re claiming evolution isn’t a factor and every species was created as is, there is no tree; every species is an unrelated dot.
