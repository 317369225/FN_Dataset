Is the phylogenetic tree of life an accurate model? I'm looking to have a version of it tattooed on my back (see link for an example) but want to be sure that its a proven theory.
If you want anything definitive, forget it. The "tree" is an inference from information that is constantly changing, and interpretations from people who change their minds and argue, sometimes cooly, often passionately, and frequently bitterly. There is not much doubt about the outlines of the various schemes, but details, often very major and important details that are the products of lifetimes of work, are always changing.
