When will it be too late to stop Global Warming?
If we discovered a sustainable, clean source of cheap energy tomorrow and switched to it as quickly as possible, warming would now continue for many decades to come. Continuing with business as usual, we are already past the point of no return. We best learn how to terraform planets quickly, because we're going to need to do it here on Earth thanks to our own shortsightedness and greed.
