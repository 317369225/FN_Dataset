How can you get job in Google after 3 years of passing college if you are an average coder and know some algorithms and some good data structures?
You can list some of the interesting projects you have developed during the time in your resume.If you are a part of some reputed company ,you can develop some internal connections in the Google employee network.These people can help in referring you for a job.There are numerous other ways to get noticed like getting actively involved in open source or excelling at some contest organised by Google(not possible for an average coder).You can also try to get an internship in Google ,which would open future prospects for a job. Apart from these ,you don't have much edge over any new graduate who is very good at programming.
