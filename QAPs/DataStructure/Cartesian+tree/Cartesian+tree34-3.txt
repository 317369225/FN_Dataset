What is your favorite tree?
Our national fruit, utterly delicious, widely available in my native Vijayawada, Mango (Magnifera Indica) tree is my favourite.... Widely available in this summer, in a wide range of varieties, the KING of all fruits is worth eating. The Banganpalli mangoes widely available in Vijayawada: In several cultures, the fruit and leaves are ritually used as floral decorations at weddings, public celebrations and religious ceremonies.
