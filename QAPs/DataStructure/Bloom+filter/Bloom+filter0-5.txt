What are the best applications of Bloom filters?
Facebook uses bloom filters for typeahead search, to fetch friends and friends of friends to a user typed query. The bloom filter is just 16 bits per friend connection (an edge in the facebook social graph) and they have called it "world's smallest bloom" :P !. The talk describes  Typeahead Search Tech Talk (6/15/2010) how it works.
