What does AOL get with the about.me acquisition?
With millions of active account users, AOL can offer them a stylish, free, and customizable personal web page done in a way that a 7-year old or 70-year old could navigate and edit with ease. And, it's fun. As these splash pages spread, they could benefit in numerous ways, mainly building out a smaller social graph to complement its email, AIM, and other messaging and content platforms.
