Linguistic Relativity: If language influences thinking, what is the "best" language to complement English?
Whatever language you can really fully immerse yourself in. If you can't develop the ability to think in your new language, you can't learn the new perspective you seek. An interesting complement might be French, for the merits of learning a language that, in contrast to English, is highly regulated and retains many traits that could be considered vestigial.
