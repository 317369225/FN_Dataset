How can intelligent people be gullible?

It purportedly showed Obamacare enrollment on an inverted Y-axis to mislead the audience into thinking enrollment was declining when it was actually increasing. She used this graph as an example of how biased and misleading Fox News is. I was immediately suspicious of the graph. Not because it inverted the Y-axis, but because it referenced CBO projections as “dates” and appeared to reverse them. It made no sense to me, even as an intentionally misleading graph.
