I think a LOT of human unhappiness is created when people write “covert contracts” with other people, whether they are strangers, family members, friends or acquaintances. What is a covert contract? It’s when you decide, on your own, that you’ll do certain things, and automatically expect the other party to reciprocate or respond. Do you say, “you’re welcome” under your breath, or even out loud when you held a door open for someone and they didn’t say thanks or even acknowledge that you are there?
