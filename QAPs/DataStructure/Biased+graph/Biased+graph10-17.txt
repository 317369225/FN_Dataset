What kind of programmer will be in shortage in the future?
Good programmers.  I'm being serious.  Good programmers have always been in shortage, and always will. In what I think is the spirit of the question, I will say that low-level programmers will be in greater shortage in the future.  Eventually, the supply will meet the demand, but there will be a shortage for a while.
