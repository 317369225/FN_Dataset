What are 20 questions to detect fake data scientists? The answers should contain math formulas or a few lines of code, not just vague generalizations.
Data scientists come in so many different flavours. It’s totally naive to believe there is one set of questions that will fit all. Which ‘flavour’ of data scientist you actually need totally depends on your project requirements. Important questions to ask yourself - not the data scientist - are: What are the business requirements for the data analysis? Which team will the data scientist be working with and which communicative requirements derive from that?
