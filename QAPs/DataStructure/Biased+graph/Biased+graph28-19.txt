What is an assumption that people often wrongly make?
That we know what we're talking about. And that we can tell who knows what they're talking about. Good luck with that! Perception is a crapshoot. We can easily manipulate it and are manipulated just as easily. Understanding is only surface deep, no matter how deep that surface may be. We're stuck in a place, in a time, in a body, in a language, in a situation, all mostly not of our choosing. Reality is imagination land built with aspiration and perspiration.
