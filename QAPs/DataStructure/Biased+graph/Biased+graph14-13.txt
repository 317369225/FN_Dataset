How do I choose the right job?
To start with, I believe that there’s no such thing as the right job. It’s you who can make it right based on your attitude because no matter where you work, there will always be issues. Racism, office politics, the lack of proper compensation or recognition and more will always be there in varying degrees. Obviously, those that are worst and may be deemed illegal should be reported to proper authorities. Those which can be managed can be dealt with as a professional.
