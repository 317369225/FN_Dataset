Who are the foremost roboticists in the world and what are some of the projects that they are working on right now?
Any current list has to include Dennis Hong at Virginia Tech, who just brought the Robocup championship to the US for the first time. And Gerd Hirzinger, director of the DLR Institute of Mechatronics and Robotics. The Institute is one of the biggest robotics research groups in the world. It has flown multiple robotics experiments on the Space Shuttle and Space Station. Hirzinger's group is probably the foremost group in the world at designing robotic mechanisms.
