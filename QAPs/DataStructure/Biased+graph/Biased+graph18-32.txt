Why do Trump supporters (81%) feel life is worse for them today than it would have been 50 years ago?
Although most humans have a predilection toward optimism, most Americans today have concluded that we are on the wrong track, that things were better in the past, and that things will be worse in the future. A big factor is the media, which amplies the unpleasant and romanticizes the past. However, most people are influenced by, more than anything else, their own experiences and those of people close to them.
