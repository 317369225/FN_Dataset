What kind of programmer will be in shortage in the future?
Fortran programmers who know numerical methods will be in shortage. There is a limited market, but still, the scientific programming community sits on an absolutely enormous amount of numerical Fortran code which has already been written, tested, and remains trusted after producing correct results for 20+ years.
