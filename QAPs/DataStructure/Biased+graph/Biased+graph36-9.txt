What are some 'must experience' classes at IIT Delhi? Do not tell the compulsory first year classes, but some classes where you were mesmerised by the teacher's in depth knowledge, or the way of teaching, or the subject itself.

But he presents them in such a way, you will love it! He gives you all his original slides for preparation. All his slides are complete in terms of content, description and animation and transition. He doesn't even shy away from doing live experiments in class! To demonstrate the fracture mechanics in solids, he brought balloons for the entire class, had us all blow one and burst them!
