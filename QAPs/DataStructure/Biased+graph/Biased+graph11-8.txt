What are the most harmful special interests in the US?
An important point here is that "special interest," in the context of US politics, generally means "anyone whose interests are contrary to mine." Depending where you stand, anyone who wants anything at all from government can be a "special interest." The only groups that escape this are mythical agglomerations that do not exist as organized groups at all, and so any group can claim their support. A good example of this is "the middle class".
