Aspie Quiz versus Autism Quotient: If you've taken the 150-question Aspie Quiz how do your composite scores compare with the 50-item AQ?
I've taken both these tests several times, and each time my scores change. Trouble is, I don't know how to answer many of the questions, and so I'm forced to do a mental coin-flip. The way I feel on a particular day may bias the flip one way or another. Mostly, this is based on the fact that I'm nearly 50 and have been problem-solving my aspie issues for decades, especially the social issues. I don't (and probably never will) have the gut-level response to social data that non-aspies have.
