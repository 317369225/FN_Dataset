What is an assumption that people often wrongly make?
Assumptions, assumptions, everywhere! That being well-schooled and being well-educated are one and the same thing Our education system is churning out students whose prime focus is to score well in tests rather than learning. The job of schools should be to teach students to ask the right questions, and then set them free to explore for the answers, instead of encouraging rote learning of facts. Neil deGrasse Tyson rightly remarked that when Students cheat on exams it's because our School System values grades more than Students value learning.
