Are Marvel fans inflating movie reviews on IMDb? I was going through reviews of recent releases of both DC and Marvel. I saw a trend whereby Marvel movie reviews tend to be extremely positive while DC is on the other side.
No, there is no pro-Marvel conspiracy on IMDb. It’s just that Marvel has put in the hard work of creating an encompassing movie universe starting in 2008, with Iron Man and their successive installments of Thor, Hulk, Captain America, Ant-Man, Guardians of the Galaxy, Doctor Strange and The Avengers. Marvel’s cinematic dominance is only recent history. DC had the lead on Marvel with Superman back in 1978 and later, Tim Burton’s Batman in 1989.
