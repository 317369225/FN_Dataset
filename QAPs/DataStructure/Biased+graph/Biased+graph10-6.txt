What kind of programmer will be in shortage in the future?
The biggest shortage will be people that can handle both hardware AND software. Developing for hardware requires a whole lot more diligence as debugging hardware that drives around, integrates in your clothes, or sits on another planet. The problem is not only debugging, but also understanding the physical principles that drive sensors, actuators, and communication. Consider an autonomous car for example.
