What tools do data scientists use for professional presentations (e.g. diagrams, graphs, flow charts, etc.)?
This is my usual toolkit (and some examples). I have a bias towards pretty, simple, and colorful presentations. Presentations: I appreciate Keynote (Mac only) because of the relative ease in making simple, quick presentations. Once you get to understand how to use it to make graphics, you should be able to make clean, professional looking presentations with great ease. Reports: This can vary a lot - common tools involve LaTeX, Markdown, Jupyter notebooks, Google Documents, or Quip Diagrams: I find Omnigraffle (Mac only) really easy to use.
