Why do Trump supporters (81%) feel life is worse for them today than it would have been 50 years ago?
You’ve asked the question the wrong way around. It’s not that Trump supporters think that life was better than 50 years ago. It’s that people who think that life was better 50 years ago are supporting Trump. People who think that the country is going in the wrong direction will vote for the option that is clearly against the status quo. We saw a similar phenomenon during the Brexit vote. Net opinions on “is life in Britain better or worse than 30 years ago?” Remain voters said “better”, by a net 46%; Leave voters said “worse”, by a net 16%.
