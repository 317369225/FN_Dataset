Analogy is not equivalence. When PETA compares animal cruelty to slavery, it simply draws an analogy between the ethical disconnect which was used to justify the enslavement of other humans, and the oppressive mindset humans have towards members of other species today. It is an analogy, not a moral equivalence and definitely not an equivalence (as an NAACP spokesperson put it) “between chickens and black people” for crying out loud.
