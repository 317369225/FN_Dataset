What can I learn/know right now in 10 minutes that will be useful for the rest of my life?
1. Did you know you can use F4 in MS Word to repeat the last action? I recently came across this. You can repeat most commands and actions by pressing F4. This is much more useful than you might think. For example, apply borders to a table. Go to your next table, select it, and press F4to apply the same borders. (Or do the same with rows within a table). Convert a picture from Floating to Inline, then use F4 to do the same with all other pictures.... (more)
