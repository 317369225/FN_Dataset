What's one of the first skills these men were taught to help them survive? How to tie a square knot. It is one of the best knots to tie two things together (you'll quickly recognize how difficult this is to do in a survival situation). You can make a rope longer, lash pieces of equipment together and do pretty much anything else with it. It also is a basic knot which is used to tie more complicated knots, so you'll have to know how to tie this one first.
