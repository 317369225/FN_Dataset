How can intelligent people be gullible?
How can intelligent people be gullible? Intelligent people make mistakes just as anyone else. Somehow we have gotten to the point of thinking intelligence means mistake-proof. Or that intelligence means being an expert in every subject. Einstein was terrible with money and struggled financially. Gates doesn't know how to remove an appendix and doesn't care to try. Jobs wore the same thing every day because he had no fashion sense, nor wanted to learn.
