How can intelligent people be gullible?
As a reasonably intelligent person, I know when I’m being conned. Almost instinctively, a little red light goes on in my mind. But that’s partly because I’ve done my share of being conned, and because I’m smart, I’ve learned from my mistakes. Unfortunately, just being smart gives no immunity to greed, misplaced trust, and the “something for nothing” delusion that form the basis of most successful cons. 134 Views
