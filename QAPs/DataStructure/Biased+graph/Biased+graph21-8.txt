What tools do data scientists use for professional presentations (e.g. diagrams, graphs, flow charts, etc.)?
There are many, and not just for data scientists. Many people versed in LaTeX find no harm in using it - its beamer template is a ready alternative to Powerpoint, and allows for the presentation of beautiful mathematics and easy figures without fuss. Data scientists used to programming R may find themselves attracted to Sweave, which allows them to embed R code in LaTeX and generate R-style figures on the fly.
