What is an assumption that people often wrongly make?
Reasons always exist. If some phenomena occurs, we may be correct in assuming that there is a cause for the phenomena. This includes human behavior. However, we cannot assume that a person her/himself is aware of the causes of her/his own behavior. This may extend to assuming that a person is responsible in some way for her/his own behavior. That is not always the case. I shall call those type of causes reasons. eg. Brett does not like cheese. Brett does not have a reason for that.
