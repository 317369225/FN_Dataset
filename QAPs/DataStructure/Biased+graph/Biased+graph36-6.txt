What are some 'must experience' classes at IIT Delhi? Do not tell the compulsory first year classes, but some classes where you were mesmerised by the teacher's in depth knowledge, or the way of teaching, or the subject itself.
Science, Technology & Human Development by Prof. Ambuj Sagar: Probably the max-fighter hukka of IIT. As the name suggests, this course exposes you to major human development challenges and how science & technology can play a role in tackling them. Every reading, lecture or tutorial will teach you something new. Professor is god. He compels you to think before you ask and analyze before you answer.
