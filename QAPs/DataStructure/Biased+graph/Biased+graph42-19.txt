What is something that needs to be said?
What is something that needs to be said? The (single/double) quotation mark comes after the punctuation mark that otherwise ends the sentence. (however also see EDIT 2 below) Let us take an example: Jimmy said, “I love you, Mommy.” That's the right way to write it, and not Jimmy said, “I love you, Mommy”. Simple, isn't it?
