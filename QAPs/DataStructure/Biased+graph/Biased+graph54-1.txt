Which cognitive bias do you struggle most with?
I feel good writing good answers on Quora, and especially when they are endorsed by others. We feel good, giving good answers. And we feel good, receiving good answers. Likewise, I feel a kind of satisfaction when I find intellectual solution to my problem. One day I discovered that this intellectual satisfaction is often the end of the process. I think about a problem — I find a solution — oh, good! And then problems continue. Nothing has changed in my life. Now tell me: what will my answer change in your life?
