How do I choose the right job?
Imagine a graph where the vertices are jobs, and there’s an edge from Job A to Job B if it is easy to get job A with job B on your resume. A decent heuristic is choosing the jobs with the highest PageRank. Effectively, what this says is that Jobs become valuable if they help you get other jobs. The most valuable jobs are jobs that help you get lots of other jobs, especially if they help you get valuable jobs. But what if there are some jobs you just like more than others?
