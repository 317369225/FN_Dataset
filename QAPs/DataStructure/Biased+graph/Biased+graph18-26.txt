Why do Trump supporters (81%) feel life is worse for them today than it would have been 50 years ago?
Life was simpler back then. I’m not a Trump fan, but I see where people are coming from. Economically: Many families were able to get by with one person working. It wasn’t always glorious, but often both parents weren’t working while someone else raised their children. We were past the “company store” and “Ludlow Massacre” days where entire families (including children) had to work or risk being shot.
