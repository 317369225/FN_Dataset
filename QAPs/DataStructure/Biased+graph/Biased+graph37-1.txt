What do people think of the TensorFlow sucks article?
One reason why there has been a huge ML/AI wave over the last few years is open source packages which have democratized Machine Learning. Tools like Scikit Learn have made ML accessible to everyone and have allowed (or even empowered) novice data scientists to do some amazing work in the field. Tensorflow, by no means, has that flavor to it. It has a hostile interface, is really difficult to install on most machines and does some weird black magic every time you do “sess.run”.
