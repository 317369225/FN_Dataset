What are the most harmful special interests in the US?
Special Interests were originally understood as corporations, especially monopolistic corporations acting against the public good. It was the genius of Fox News, and later Republicans, to redefine special interest as teachers unions, the ACLU, National Public Radio, and those consumer and regulatory agencies previously identified by the public as those protecting them from special interests.
