What tools do data scientists use for professional presentations (e.g. diagrams, graphs, flow charts, etc.)?
Primarily, we use tools that produce output that the client can manage.  This tends toward PowerPoint (diagrams and flow-charts) and Excel (graphs).  Sometimes, Visio is the tool of choice for flow-charts and other diagrams.  If there's a plot that Excel doesn't do (rare in materials we present to clients), we'll use SPSS, R, or Salford Predictive Miner (tool we generally use for ML work). For internal work (e.g. data exploration and preparation for a presentation) we do graphs in the native platform (SPSS, R, SPM).  The graphs are easy to generate directly in these tools.
