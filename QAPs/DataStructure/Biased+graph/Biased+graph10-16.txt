What kind of programmer will be in shortage in the future?
As far as I can tell there always has been and there always will be a shortage of 'scientific' programmers. By that I mean - programmers which understand (or can quickly learn) the 'science' used in programs (e.g. statistical or diff. eq. models) - programmers which are at least somewhat language agnostic (at least within one programming paradigm).
