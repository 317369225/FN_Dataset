How can intelligent people be gullible?
Because being intelligent doesn’t exclude being gullible. People are more irrational than they would like to accept. Intelligent people tend to rationalize and invent incredible excuses. Also, “school smart” people often lack real life experience, so their intellect is almost a disadvantage to them as they sometimes genuinely believe in laughable things but their sheltered world doesn’t allow facts to disprove their beliefs.
