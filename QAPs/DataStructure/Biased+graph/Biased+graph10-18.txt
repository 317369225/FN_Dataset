What kind of programmer will be in shortage in the future?
Competent and efficient ones. They have a much more conservative and efficient approach for solving the problems, and can usually get much more bang for the buck. Newer developers grown up in the middle of "waste". Need more performance? Add more GHz, add more CPUs, add more memory, things are cheap. And software gets bloated.
