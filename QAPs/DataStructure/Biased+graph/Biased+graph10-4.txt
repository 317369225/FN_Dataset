What kind of programmer will be in shortage in the future?
There's no shortage of programmers, and there won't be a shortage of good programmers.  But there will always be a shortage of socially validated programmers. Regardless of how many coders there are, only a fixed number of college students can go to top schools and get top tier internships.  If you don't have that pedigree it's hard to be interviewed at top companies as a new grad, even if you're very good.  Similarly, there are O(1) slots at top companies, and working at a mediocre place now will make it harder to get a top-tier job later.
