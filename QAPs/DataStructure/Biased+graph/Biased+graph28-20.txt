What is an assumption that people often wrongly make?
The Fundamental Attribution Error is something I see ALL THE TIME. ------------------------ As a simple example, if Alice saw Bob trip over a rock and fall, Alice might consider Bob to be clumsy or careless ([attributing the fall to a flaw that Bob has]). If Alice tripped over the same rock herself, she would be more likely to blame the placement of the rock ([attributing the fall to the situation]). (Wikipedia) ... (more)
