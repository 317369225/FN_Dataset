Where can I find examples of a U-Squared distribution?
These distributions are common in dosage relationships for drugs and food.  For example, some but not all studies of red meat consumption v mortality show that a little is better than none but eating a lot is worse.  Alcohol consumption shows similar u-shaped curves in some populations.  Similar relationships obviously hold for drug dosage: the optimum dose is, well, optimum; none or an overdose is worse.
