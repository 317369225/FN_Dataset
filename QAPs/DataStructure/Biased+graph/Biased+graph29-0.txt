As Tony Robinson and Manish Saraswat have already pointed out, "fake data scientist" is loosely defined at best, elitist at worst. Most of the times I've seen that term bandied about in Internet discussions, the de facto definition seems to be "someone who knows less about data science than I do", which makes it meaningless. Nobody talks about "fake biologists" or "fake physicists" so why is "fake data scientists" a legitimate label?
