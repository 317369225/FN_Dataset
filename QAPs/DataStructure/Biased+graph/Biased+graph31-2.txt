Hello, I am on the Orient Technologies team, so please consider this natural bias up front. First, thanks for considering OrientDB. You have recognized our most prominent design goal - to create an operational grade database that covers the largest use case possible without compromising speed. As you've seen on our website, we are a document-graph database, meaning you can work with OrientDB very similar to MongoDB as a document database or as a native graph database.
