How can intelligent people be gullible?
It happens very often. Highly intelligent people tend to believe in almost laughable ideas and theories. Let me elaborate on the issue. Intelligence is the ability to grasp complex ideas and situations. That has the side effect of training the intelligent brain to see complexity where there is none. “Confirmation bias” starts getting into play when intellectuals see non-existent complexity.
