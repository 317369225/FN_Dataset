How do I choose the right job?
I’m not sure if you’re a student, a teen, maybe you’re an adult or graduate student. But if you do happen to be a student or teen, try using your tech-savvy skills to find information about jobs online. There’s some excellent websites (like CareerFear, The Student Room etc. ) that give you engaging, real life information about jobs and various careers to help you make the most informed decision you can.
