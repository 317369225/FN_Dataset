What are some 'must experience' classes at IIT Delhi? Do not tell the compulsory first year classes, but some classes where you were mesmerised by the teacher's in depth knowledge, or the way of teaching, or the subject itself.
Most of the courses here are either in computer science or mathematics department. Being a student of chemical engineering, one of the most amazing courses is the one taken by Professor Balakrishana Pitchumani. He does not take any specific course but whichever course he takes he makes it a point to include all topics in chemical engineering and specifically points out the topics left out in most courses.
