How do I choose the right job?
This is a very difficult question, but I will try to help you sort things out a bit. The most important thing to realize is that you do NOT have to make any decisions about "choosing a right path" immediately, and that even if you make some choices that you decide later on are not the best choices for you, you can change your mind, go in a different direction entirely, and it will not have been a waste of time.
