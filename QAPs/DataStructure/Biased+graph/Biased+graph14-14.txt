How do I choose the right job?
It depends on your blood type! Some people have the throb of youth in the veins, all their lives. They move mountains. Some people don’t like stress; it affects their blood pressure. They stay in the shallows. Some people warm up in sunshine. When it is cold and dark they slink away to hide in the shadows. It all depends what you are prepared to do to get to the ‘right job’. I have a half-formulated hypothesis. I have typecast people in broad classes: bear with me. Some people are ‘current lovers’: they are likes leaves on a river.
