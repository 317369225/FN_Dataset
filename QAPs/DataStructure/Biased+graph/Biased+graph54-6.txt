Which cognitive bias do you struggle most with?
Curse of Knowledge, baby. I am prone to think what is obvious to me should be obvious to others. It’s a very long time since I brought all my questions together, and began to function at the gnostic level; after thirty years of building on that scaffolding, imagining the modal existence is like imagining the mind of an alien. This is one of the benefits of getting involved in theatre. Character work. The hard-easy effect, where I tend to see the form of the solution immediately on encountering the problem definition, and feel like I’m done when there is a month of work to do.
