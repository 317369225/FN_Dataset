What can you not fathom, understand, or reconcile no matter how hard you try?
We have no free will. Scientific and rational inquiry into the nature of the brain and behavior tells us that all of our thoughts and experiences and behaviors and choices are the result of myriad interacting physical processes. We live in a material universe, subject to and the result of an unending web of cause and effect - there is no place for the pause or gap of "will." I can intellectually accept that, but it seems to be impossible to truly accept and understand - the perception of free will is simply part of our inescapable perceptual machinery.
