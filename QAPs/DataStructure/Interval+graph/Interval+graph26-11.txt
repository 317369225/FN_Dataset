What are some brain hacks that a neuroscientist or a psychologist knows that most people don't?
Willpower is a Limited Resource. It becomes depleted throughout the day and replenished with rest, sleep, and glucose -  hence why we crave sweets and carbs when we are tired and stressed out. Handle your most challenging tasks early in the day and least cognitively and physically taxing ones as you fatigue.
