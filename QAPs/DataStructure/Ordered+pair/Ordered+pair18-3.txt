Let [math]a[/math] and [math]b[/math] both be positive integers less than or equal to 2015. For how many distinct ordered pairs [math](a,b)[/math] is the following expression also an integer? [math]\frac{a^2+b^2}{13}[/math]
Reed answer assumes that [math]b \ge a[/math]. This may be what you want if [math]a[/math] and [math]b[/math] form an unorderd pair. On the other hand, if [math](a,b)[/math] is considered an ordered pair, that is if [math](1,5)[/math] is not the same as [math](5,1)[/math] then thera are 600625 posibilities: >>> len(filter(lambda x:int(x)==x,[(a*a+b*b)/13.0 for a in xrange(1,2016) for b in xrange(1,2016)])) 600625 >>>
