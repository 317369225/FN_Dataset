What were the greatest intellectual achievements of the 20th century in science and mathematics?
One of the greatest scientific/mathematical achievements in the 20th Century was the formulation of Information Theory and the association of information with Entropy by Claude Shannon in 1948. It has formed the foundation for the world's vast communications infrastructure by helping to better understand the limits of signal bandwidth, power, encoding and processing.
