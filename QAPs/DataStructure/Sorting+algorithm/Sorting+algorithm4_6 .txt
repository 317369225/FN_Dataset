However, you can do better with UnShuffle sort (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UnS...). UnShuffle sort uses a data structure called Pile (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pil.... Basically, you will creating Pile collection based on the streaming numbers. Each pile is created as list:- where an item may be added to the head of the list if the new item is valued less than or equal to the current head or to the tail of the list if the new item is greater than or equal to the current tail. Elements may be removed from both the head and the tail. Then you can merge the multiple pile collection to get the sorted numbers.
