Quicksort: What is the quick sort algorithm?Quick sort is fast, in place , unstable. In Quick sort you select a random element (There are different strategies to select Pivot) as Pivot, then you move all the element greater than the pivot to its right and less than the pivot to its left. So basically you have placed the Pivot at its final position in sorted array. You repeat this till array is sorted Worst case is n^2, avg case is n log n
