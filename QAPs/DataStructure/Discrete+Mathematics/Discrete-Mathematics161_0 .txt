Let me take your recurrence relation as an example. This is what a generating function is used for. To determine our generating function, we start from a n =4+12 a n−1   an=4+12an−1 and we multiply both sides by x n   xn : a n x n =4x n +12 a n−1 x n .  anxn=4xn+12an−1xn. This method looks complicated and messy, but its main advantage is that it works for the majority of recurrence relations, whether they are simple or complicated.
