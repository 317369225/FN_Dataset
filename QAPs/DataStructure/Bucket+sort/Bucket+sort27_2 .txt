Both bucket-sort and insertion-sort often come in handy for various little tasks when cleaning up/organizing stuff. Recursive binary search is all right for finding entries in long lists or catalogues in alphabetic order. Tour-leaders, airplane crews, schoolteachers, and others who are responsible for not forgetting any individuals, are frequently heavy users of various barrier algorithms. I'd love to say that I plan how to get to places using shortest path, but in reality, I don't. I'm sure there are people who do, though.
