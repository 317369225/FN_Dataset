What are the best graduate programs for HCI in the United States and Europe?
Some of the top names (not in any particular order) that come to my mind. Stanford University (http://hci.stanford.edu/) MIT Media Lab (http://www.media.mit.edu/) Human Computer Interaction Institute (http://www.hcii.cmu.edu/) at Carnegie Mellon University School of Information at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (https://www.si.umich.edu/academi...) School of Information at the University of Washington, Seattle (http://dub.washington.edu/) iSchool and the Berkeley Institute of Design (BiD) at the University of California, Berkeley (http://hci.berkeley.edu/) Georgia Institute of Technology (http://www.cc.gatech.edu/hci/) University of Minnesota, Twin Cities (http://www.cs.umn.edu/research/r...) University of California, Irvine (http://hci.ics.uci.edu/HCC_and_I...) Center for Human Computer Interaction at Virginia Tech (http://www.hci.vt.edu/index.php) Also University of Toronto (http://www.dgp.toronto.edu/), University of British Columbia, and University of Waterloo have reputed professors working in the field of HCI and ICTD.
