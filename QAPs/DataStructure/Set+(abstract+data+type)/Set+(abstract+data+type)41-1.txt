Did Steve Jobs often send mass emails to Apple employees? If so, what were they about?
Steve didn't write nearly as many emails to the staff as Tim Cook does... but my favorite was when Apple passed Dell in valuation. Michael Dell had said a few years before about Apple - "I'd shut it down and give the money back to the shareholders." Obviously... things changed, and when Apple stock was on the rise, Steve wrote: "Team, it turned out that Michael Dell wasn't perfect at predicting the future. Based on today's stock market close, Apple is worth more than Dell. Stocks go up and down, and things may be different tomorrow, but I thought it was worth a moment of reflection today. Steve."
