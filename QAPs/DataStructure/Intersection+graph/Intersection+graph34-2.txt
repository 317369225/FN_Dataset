What are some of the best stories about life at IIM Ahmedabad?
There is this one story narrated by the Bangalore chapter head of IIM A at the recently held Synchrony meet: A guy in their batch received just 1/2 a mark on his quiz. So he walked up to the professor and asked him,"Sir what is this 1/2 mark out of, out of 10, 20, 30, what? The professor calmly replied, "This 1/2 mark is out of sympathy, son!"
