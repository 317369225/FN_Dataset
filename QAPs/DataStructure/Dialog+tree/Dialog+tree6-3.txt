What qualities of Mass Effect 2 make it an exceptional game?
As a big fan of the Mass Effect series, here's what I feel makes ME2 a great experience: 1. Combat: Extremely engaging, fun and leaves you with wanting more. 2. Characters: The plot helps the player get to know the characters in detail to an extent, their backgrounds, etc. which by the end leaves you with a sense of attachment. 3. Plot: A very good plot combined with the other elements including the scale , brilliant visuals, twists and turns in the story, etc. make it an experience like no other. 4. Ending: And most importantly, the Ending. The end of the game must leave the player satisfied, moved and bestow him with a sense of achievement. Mass Effect 2 does an exceptional job here.
