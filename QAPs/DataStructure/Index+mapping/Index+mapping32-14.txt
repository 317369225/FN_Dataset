What tools do you use to monitor buzz about your startup?You know, besides the obvious like google alerts, etc...
I think Webfluenz www.webfluenz.com is quite good. The free version is a little buggy, but still displays features such a heat-maps of where people are discussing topics and gender breakdown of those involved in the conversations. For longer-term tracking, Netvibes.com dashboards are a great way to see what is getting posted and discussed. Their paid version even offers analytics and trend reports.
