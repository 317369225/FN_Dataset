What are memory maps? How does one use them for enhancing learning ability?
Visualising your thoughts is great idea to remember them. Mind mapping is a highly effective way to store information in your brain. The creative way of note taking. Mind map is like a map of a city. The city center represents the main Idea, main roads represent the key points of Idea and secondary roads are secondary Ideas. To make it more memorable use more colors, shapes, images. Use it to unlock the dynamic potential of your brain. You can draw mind map of your idea or plan on paper to remember that in order. There are softwares and mobile apps to create those maps.
