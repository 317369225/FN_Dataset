If the Sun's gravity is constantly pulling planets toward it, why hasn't the Earth been pulled into the Sun?
Robert Frost gives an excellent summary of how planets maintain orbits.  Right now Earth has a stable orbit around the sun.  However, the Earth is not expected to maintain its orbit forever.  In about 7.59 billion years the Sun will enter its red giant phase, expanding its atmosphere to engulf the Earth and other inner planets,  causing them to spiral inward.
