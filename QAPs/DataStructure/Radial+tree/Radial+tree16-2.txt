What is the greatest mystery of all time?
So you studied this in school, didn't you? Ever wondered how the hell did it disappear? I have. But no answer. Indus Valley Civilization. The Population was more than 5 million. Stretched from India to Afghanistan. No one knows how they disappeared. And the excavations in 1922 reveal a surprisingly hygienic and organized lifestyle. In fact, it is said that their sanitary and piping systems were better than what we have today. While people do suggest violence as the end, there is no archaeological evidence of armies, slaves, social conflicts or other vices prevalent in ancient societies. Even to the very end, it seems, they kept it clean. How did they maintain this? It is almost an impossible task. And yet, they disappeared. Another impossible task. For more: Page on Thinkquest
