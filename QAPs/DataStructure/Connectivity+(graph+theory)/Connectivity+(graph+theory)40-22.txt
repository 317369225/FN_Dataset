Originally Answered: When studying math, how can I relate it to music?Here is a great example of what kind of music to listen while studying math: When switching from a reading-based task to math, it might be wise to switch from jazz to "mood calming" music. A 2002 University of London study found second graders who were listening to soothing music while doing math problems finished a mean of 34.9 problems at an accuracy rate  of 84 percent in 15 minutes, compared to 27.3 problems at an accuracy rate of 80 percent for those who did not listen to music.
