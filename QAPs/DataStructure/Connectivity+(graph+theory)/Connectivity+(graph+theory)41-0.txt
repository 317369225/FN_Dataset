Are there any mathematics studies higher than calculus? If so, which ones?
Calculus is the highest level course in a high school curriculum, but that's exactly like being the biggest fish in a very small pond.  Really, it's basic math that everyone who does anything mathy at all knows. If you were studying math at the college level, you'd probably finish up your calculus classes by the end of your freshman year and then go on to study things like linear algebra, probability, complex analysis, group theory or number theory.  If you were to go on to do graduate work, you'd then discover that all of those are basic math that everyone knows....
