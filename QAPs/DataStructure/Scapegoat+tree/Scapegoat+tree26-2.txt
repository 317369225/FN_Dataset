What are the most beautiful lyrics you have ever heard?
Roger Waters wrote "Time" in his late 20s. It appears on Pink Floyd's 1973 album Dark Side Of The Moon. The lyrics are evocative and regardless of how reckless one feels or what age one is, reach deep inside. I first heard this when I was 17. APP (they had an active role in the arrangement on this song) was already my favourite band by far by then. And including the haunting Clare Torry solo at the end, the song has everything going for it.
