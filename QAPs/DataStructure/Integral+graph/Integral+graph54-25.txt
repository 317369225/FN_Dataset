What should all first time entrepreneurs know before starting their very first business?
Don't listen old conventional norms. Often teachers, parents and people not related to entrepreneurship try to stop young entrepreneurs from starting business. Their excuses are that you are too young, you must graduate college, you most work at least 5 years in corporate business etc. But if you have a dream, if you have a vision and a lot of motivation - start immediately!
