What will be the next big trend on the Internet after "search" of Google and "social" of Facebook?
The Block chain (database) technology of Bitcoin has the potential to be the next big thing as its applications are very broad. Distributed systems with no central core that not only governments but even their own founder cannot censor or destroy - this is the only digital freedom in a global sense. Projects like https://ethereum.org/ will change the world forever for better or worse.
