What will be the next big trend on the Internet after "search" of Google and "social" of Facebook?
The next big trend will be Software Provenance, the foundation for making the applications people use be reliable and work together.   This includes basic trust, i.e. that a particular piece of software has known privacy implications and is not Malware.
