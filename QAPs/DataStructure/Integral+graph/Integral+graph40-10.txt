What are some promising Big Data startups?Including both funded and non-funded ones. And maybe some OSS apps or stacks as well. (A startup doesn't always have to be a company, does it?)
Recommender systems have become an intrinsic and important aspect of everyday big data.  Myrrix(http://www.myrrix.com) from the developer of recommendation framework in mahout is a  real-time and  scalable recommender system for Big data, built on Apache Mahout. Cloudera(http://www.cloudera.com)  develops open-source software for a world dependent on Big Data. It is the leader in Apache Hadoop-based software and services led by veteran Doug Cutting himself
