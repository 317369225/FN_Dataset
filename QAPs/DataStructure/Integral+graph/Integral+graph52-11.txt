Definitely a threat to LinkedIn's main Recruiter product for a number of different reasons: a) LinkedIn really only allows to recruit white collar employees. Facebook covers everything (blue collar, graduates, etc). b) LinkedIn is expensive ($10 for an InMail vs. $1 for a Facebook Graph Search message). c) LinkedIn does not allow recruiters to generate referrals, Facebook Graph Search does (e.g. a recruiter at Google could search for: "Friends of people at Google who are Software Engineers at Facebook").
