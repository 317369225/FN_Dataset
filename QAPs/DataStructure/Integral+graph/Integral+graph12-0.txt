Do you think Facebook open graph integration to apps is making them a spam?Example the social reading apps, trending videos etc.
Using facebook open graph actions isn't a trivial procedure, while it can be huge for marketing your application among your facebook user base. Staying current with the best practices and dev integration can be a full time job, especially if you don't want to come off as spam.  SaaS product Carrot is a great tool to alleviate these problems allowing you to take advantage of the open graph without being an expert. In an interview on our podcast, Mark explains how different apps might use open graph actions for their user's common and extraordinary behaviors...  Actions can be anything from reading or bookmarking an article to achieving a new level or ranking up in a game.
