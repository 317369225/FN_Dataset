How do you Explain "Abstract Data Types" in layman terms?
I will try to explain u this by giving a very common daily life example Like you went to a Cafe, you asked the guy over there to bring you a Cappuccino, After some time that guy bought you a Cappuccino and you paid for it and you went away. So now in this Example Abstract Data type is that guy that bought you the Cappuccino because he didn't tell you how he made Cappuccino he just did what you asked him to do and this is what an Abstract data type is , it just performs a specified operation and doesn't really cares about how it is implemented .
