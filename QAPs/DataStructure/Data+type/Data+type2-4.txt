What are data types in c?
There are so many examples… size_t is derived from integers. It really depends on how you mean “derived data types” as a structure definition is a derived data type (from the aggregation of the fields within the structure). Looking through the /usr/include for size_t finds it being defined in terms of other definitions - but eventually they all go back to an integer. Usually something like typedef size_t <some integer type> And that boils down into a “typedef size_t int”. Another form of derived data types are done with the “enum” keyword.
