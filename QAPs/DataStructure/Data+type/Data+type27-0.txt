Why is the C language known as rich & powerful in its data type?
Why would you consider it “rich in data types” where only types are Integer, Floating point, Character(which is actually integer), and pointers to these? Python has Numbers, strings, dictionaries, tuples, lists, classes, and many others. The perceived richness of C comes from the variants of integers, like 8-bit(char), 16-bit(short), 32-bit(long), 64-bit(long long) integers, and their signed and unsigned versions.
