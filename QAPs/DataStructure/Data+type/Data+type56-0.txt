What are the advantages of data types?
Are you referring to having to declare data types, so the advantages of static typing? If that’s the case, there are two main advantages. First, the variables are known at compile time, and the compiler will be able to catch many bugs early on. Second, other programmers will be able to follow your work more easily. Otherwise, if you’re just talking about having the concept of data types period, then the advantage is simple.
