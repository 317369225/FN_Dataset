What is data type?
They are an abstraction to represent different type of information in a “language” understandable by computers (binary). If you have a computer which understands only sequences of 0 and 1 and you want to make them perform basic math operations, then you have to create a standard for representing integer numbers and floating point numbers. How do you choose which is the best format? It depends on many factors: space occupation for example, or also the speed at which some kind of circuit can perform in hardware mode certain operations.
