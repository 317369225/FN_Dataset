What are the disadvantages of line graphs over bar graphs?
Talking about the disadvantages of line graphs over bar charts is like talking about the disadvantages of screwdrivers over spanners: they are two different tools for two different jobs, and both are useful, but for different things. Bar charts are very useful for snapshot views. They show you how different organisations, companies, schools, teams or whatever compared at particular points in time.
