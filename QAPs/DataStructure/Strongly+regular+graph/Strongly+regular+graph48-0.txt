How was your experience writing PG entrance exam (PGEE) for IIIT-H for Computer Science?
I would like to share my experiences of Admission in M.Tech. Program in IIIT Hyderabad in the year 2010. Q: How did you prepare and how was the exam ? I had prepared for GATE 2010 and I believe that was more than enough.
