Who are the most accomplished female scientists?
In no particular order: Lise Meitner Austrian nuclear physicist who was one of the discoverers of nuclear fission. Her colleague Otto Hahn was awarded the Nobel prize for the discovery. The element Meitnerium is named after her. Lisa Randall Well known for the Randall-Sundrum model in string theory. The first first tenured woman in the Princeton physics department and the first tenured female theoretical physicist at both MIT and Harvard. Émilie du Châtelet French mathematician and physicist, well known for her bold scientific ideas that were far ahead of her times. She is well known for her interpretation of Newton's Principia Mathematica and for realizing that kinetic energy is proportional to the velocity squared, and not the velocity, as had been erroneously believed by Newton, Voltaire and others.
