Who are the most accomplished female scientists?
Dorothy Hodgkin She won Noble prize in Chemistry for her work in X-ray crystallography, especially Protein crystallography. Her work includes confirming the structure of penicillin, determining the structure of insulin, vitamin B12. When Britain's royal mail decided to issue stamps on 350th anniversary of Royal Society, she was the only women among the likes of Newton and Benjamin Franklin. Stephanie Kwolek She was a chemist at Dupont who is known for discovering Kevlar.
