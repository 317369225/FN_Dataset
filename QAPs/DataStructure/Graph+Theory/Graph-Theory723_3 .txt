Matlab code t = 1:12; y = [15 14 15 18 21 25 27 26 24 20 18 16]; Fvect = exp(1i*2*pi*(1:12)/12); F0 = sum(y.*Fvect); m = mod(angle(F0)/(2*pi)*12-1,12)+1; figure; bar(t,y); hold on; a = abs(F0)/6; b = 2*pi/12; c = (m-3); d = mean(y); plot([t] , a*sin(b*(t - c)) +d, 'r', 'LineWidth', 3 ); m = 7.45 So it peaks just before the end of July.
