What are the pros and cons of bar graphs?Bar graphs are a GUI visual for executive or higher management. Either used in a Presentation, report or “macro” metric to management or other interested party. It’s not meant to be “micro” or granular. Pros and Cons: Bar graphs are they at the same time both Pro and Con, they are both limited to X-axis and Y-axis displays, so it’s 2 dimensional, E.g. it can show time based progression, or progression vs. volume Pros: great for summary, Macro, mostly for management or executives Cons: Macro not Micro, not granular, 2 dimensional, X-axis, Y-axis based, not for engineers or technical, for that use spreadsheets which are ‘micro’ and granular 189 Views
