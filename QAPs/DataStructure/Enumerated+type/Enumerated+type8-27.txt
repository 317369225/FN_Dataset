Software Engineering: What are good ways to avoid bugs while programming?
Pair-programming is an excellent way of avoiding bugs, and comes with a host of other advantages. More info: Pair programming It's also important to generally understand how your fellow teammates (not the one you are paired with) are contributing to the same codebase you're working on day-to-day, for a number of reasons, most importantly by keeping things DRY and limiting code. There is no code less buggy and faster than no code, after all. In addition, your team will likely have an expert in areas that you're not -- leveraging that fact well prevent you from repeating mistakes they may have already learned the hard way.
