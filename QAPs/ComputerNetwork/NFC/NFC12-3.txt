★Will NFC replace QR codes?
QR Codes are here to stay for some time. Even when NFC becomes a viable method of communication, affordable and widespread, QR Codes will still serve a place particularly on packaging and in magazines. What is exciting is that new ways of developing the use of QR Codes has emerged which enables the owner of the product or ad to control what is shown an when the message is displayed.
