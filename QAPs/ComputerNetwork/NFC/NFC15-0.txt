★What are great NFC use cases outside of payment?
NFC-enabled phones have the potential to replace: - the card for getting into your hotel room - your car keys - your business card - your public transportation (i.e bus, train) card - concert, sports or other event tickets - your boarding pass for getting on a plane - coupons - anything that is a card - anything in your wallet - most paper or physical forms of identification NFC can also be used to: - sync devices - share mobile apps with your friends (give someone Angry Birds) - transfer photos between cameras, smartphones, or iPads My lists are non-exhaustive.    Embed Quote
