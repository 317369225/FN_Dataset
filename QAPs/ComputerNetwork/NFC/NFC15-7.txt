★What are great NFC use cases outside of payment?
Nokia actually gave a great presentation at The Mobile Show Middle East this year about how NFC technology would be used with the Nokia N9. They showed how the device could be used as a marketing tool.   In the context of a cinema, Nokia showed how NFC technology could allow people to place their phone next to a sign to get free tickets, or free popcorn. Then, if they held their phone to another sign they would receive information about movie times etc.   So that was a really good way of showing a different side to NFC.   Tha's the basic idea but If you want more details you can actually watch the video here:    Embed Quote
