★What are some cool NFC apps?
One cool mobile app is MapYourTag. It enables you to track any of your asset, equipment, tool or whatever at ease with your mobile. Put the NFC tag on your asset, tap it with your smartphone, assign the tag to your asset and update information about your asset. MapYourTag spots your assets on a map with update information!
