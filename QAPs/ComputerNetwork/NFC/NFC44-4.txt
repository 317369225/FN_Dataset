★If NFC payments do not take off in the next 12 months, what will be the primary use of NFC on smartphones?
It will probably find applications for fidelity programs and cards, where the user does not feel the need for the same level of security as he requires for his banking informations. First advantage could be that instead of carying around a wallet with twenty membership cards, everything should be in one place - i.e. in the Smartphone.  Also, in domotics and one-to-one experience, NFC could be a way to identify customer/carier/passenger etc, and allow them to have a customized experience of a service. Let's say you own a car you and your wife use. You have your own preferences stored into your profile. With your NFC smartphone, the car identifies the driver and automatically puts on the right settings: radio stations, driver seat, etc...    Embed Quote
