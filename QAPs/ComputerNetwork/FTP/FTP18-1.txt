FTP connections are not encrypted. Thus, it would be possible for someone with a packet sniffer to eavesdrop on an FTP connection. For alternatives, you can use either SFTP or FTPS. Both support data-in-motion encryption. This article discusses the vulnerability of FTP due to the absence of encryption and how encrypted file transfer protocols like SFTP and FTPS can be used address the problem: Countering Packet Sniffers Using Encrypted FTP
