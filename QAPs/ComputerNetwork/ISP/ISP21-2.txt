★Why can't the current ISP monopoly be broken?
We have to be very clear about the state of the market. There isn't an issue with ISPs. There are issues with residential ISPs. There's actually pretty solid competition to provide Internet Service to businesses. Where the market is having trouble generating healthy competition is in delivering service to private homes. There are some very good answers relevant to this question at What is it that keeps business broadband providers from entering the residential market? The very, very short answer is the extremely high costs of the "last mile" connection and of home customer support.    Embed Quote
