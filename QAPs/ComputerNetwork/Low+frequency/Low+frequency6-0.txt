★Does NFC include low frequency and high frequency RFID?Ignore the buzzword, is "NFC" on the same frequencies that "RFID" is using today? LF is 125kHz and HF is 13.56 Mhz.
NFC is only 13.56 Mhz.  There is a "sister" frequency, 433.92 Mhz, which is the 32nd harmonic above 13.56 and allows NFC antennae and silicon (with a minor modification) to also execute longer range applications than are possible with NFC, e.g. automatic/background check-ins, reading a movie poster from many meters away, smart billboards, mobile advertising without requiring a user to "tap" on a poster/kiosk/billboard.  Check out DASH7 if you are interested ...    Embed Quote
