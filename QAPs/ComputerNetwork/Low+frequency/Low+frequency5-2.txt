★What is the relation between high or low frequencies and the sharpness of an image?
Light on the blue end (high frequency) end of the spectrum is harder to focus with a lense.   It tends to scatter. Light on the red end (low frequency) focusses more sharply. This is why airplane pilots wear amber colored sunglasses - they block out blue light which doesn't focus well. I don't know the physics behind it ...    Embed Quote
