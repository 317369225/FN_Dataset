★Java Application Servers: What are some alternatives to Jetty?
In open source, the typical alternative is Tomcat. For servlet implementations, I haven't seen any good alternatives to the embeddability of Jetty. Also worth looking at is Caucho Resin. For full application servers (full Java EE), there is Glassfish and Tomcat. At the high end, there is WebLogic, which is a commercial application server.
