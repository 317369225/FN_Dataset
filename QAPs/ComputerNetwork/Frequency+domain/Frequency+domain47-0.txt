★What advice would you give to an electrical and electronics student that you wish you were given when you started your course?All advice  are welcome!
Strengthen your physics and math fundamentals Learn how to read datasheets Learn how to learn, unlearn and relearn. Learn how to design circuit boards using some good software and have them physically made and test them (No circuit study is complete unless you have physically implemented it on the circuit board) Buy a DMM and soldering iron, and USE them Learn C Start using Linux, IRC and get involved in hardware opensource projects like http://fabathome.org, http://gnuradio.org etc... Build your own capacitor, resistor, antenna or any other electrical components using paper, clips and other locally available material Develop the hacker attitude (read about Nikola Tesla)
