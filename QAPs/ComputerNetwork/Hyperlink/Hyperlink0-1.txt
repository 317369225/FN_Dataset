★Why are hyperlinks blue?
One good reason is blue hyperlinks can be perceived by nearly everyone, while red and green color are difficult for 7-10% of men and about .4% of women due to varying degrees of color blindness. According to designer Joe Clark, in the chapter Type and Color from his book Building Accessible Websites: Red and green are the colours most affected by colour-vision deficiency.  Almost no one has a blue deficiency. Accordingly, nearly everyone can  see blue, or, more accurately, almost everyone can distinguish blue as a  colour different from others.
