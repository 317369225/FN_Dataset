Do you have SharePoint in house? If so, the FAST for SharePoint 2010 search is very powerful. Coveo also has a search engine that is very Microsoft friendly. Regardless of what tool you purchase, the important thing to realize is that managing an Enterprise Search tool takes time and resources that need to be dedicated to rank-tuning and indexing that content which you want to be found. You can turn a crawler on and bring back everything, but relevancy is key to ensuring your users find what they want when they want.
