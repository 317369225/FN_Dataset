A good corporate intranet is one that meets the needs of the business and it's users. At its best, the intranet is an integral part of the day to day working of every employee and fundamental to the companies business operations. Taking the razorfish maturity model razorfish maturity model (?) as an example, an intranet starts by offering a core communication channel, builds to employee self service offerings and MI dashboardsMI dashboards (?), and eventually becomes an integrated gateway to all employee/company/customer transactions.
