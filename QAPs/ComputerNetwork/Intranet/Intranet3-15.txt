★What are Sharepoint alternatives for a company-wide intranet?In a ~500-1000 person company, what alternatives are there for a  document, file repository-style intranet portal? Bonus points if there is room and potential for other elements of the information workplace (such as collaboration, social, content management) down the road.
I don't think that you'll have a problem finding alternatives to Sharepoint (I see you've already got looks of good suggestions!). The main problem that you're going to have is finding something that gives you the flexibility to add custom features in the future. Twine is a really good option, since, on top of the social, mobile and doc management features, you've also got the option to work with the team to develop custom features for your business later on.
