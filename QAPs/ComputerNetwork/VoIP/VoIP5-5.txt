★What is the best VoIP infrastructure service, API or open source solution to use for a mobile VoIP app?The mobile app is a SIP client.
Lots of people discussing the software stack and setup method. Commendable but all become moot points if you forget the last mile. You are totally reliant on the mobile service providers network and management. Regardless of anything fancy you do at your end make investigations. Buy a SIM card from each MNO that covers your area. Try pre pay and post pay services - they sometimes perform differently. Tracert from the SIM data connection and see if you can find the places they peer, get your server in the same place. TEST TEST TEST The last thing you need is to get your side right then be scuppered with an awful experience because the mobile network doesn't accommodate your unusual consumption habits. Remember the mobile networks are not built for VoIP    Embed Quote
