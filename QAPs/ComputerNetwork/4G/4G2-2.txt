★What is LTE (4G)?
WHAT IS LTE?LTE (Long-term evolution) is the most recent in the line of the GSM broadband network evolvement. The standard was developed by 3GPP – 3rd Generation Partnership Project. LTE offers data speeds of up to 100 Mb/s downlink and 50 Mb/s uplink. Most of the world countries have at least one LTE network. LTE is compatible with previous mobile technologies – GSM, GPRS, UMTS, EDGE, WCDMA, HSPA, CDMA etc. Better technology for power consumption of mobile terminals – it uses SC-FDMA for uplink – modulation technology that saves battery life of mobile terminalsFor more: Akshay Pandey's answer to How is LTE different from WIMAX?
