★What is "far infrared"?
Usually it's infrared from about 15,000 nm to 1,000,000 nm. The top half of the range is basically microwaves. A far infrared transmitter in that range could be similar to the gadget in a microwave oven. Infrared in the low end of that range could be generated by a plate of metal, or even wood, heated to about body temperature.    Embed Quote
