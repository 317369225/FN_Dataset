★What is the best free online service for a simple CMS?
I think the key word here is simple. If you want something for a simple website, use a simple CMS. WordPress is great but for something very basic, there are better options. I'd first consider who will be using the platform and go from there. If it's someone with little to no knowledge of web design, I might point them to Weebly or Wix since it's so simple and drag and drop based.
