★Indian Railways: Which is the best train route in India?
Many well known train journeys have been mentioned here, there are some not too well known ones, that are equally picturesque. Vizag- Araku This is actually a part of the Vizag- Kirandul rail route, and it passes through some of the most scenic locations, including Shimliguda, which at an altitude of 996 MSL, is the highest Broad Gauge Railway station. Nandyal-Giddalur line Connecting  Nandyal in Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh, with Giddalur in Prakasam district, this railway line cuts through the Nallamalla Hills, passing through some of the thickest forests.    Embed Quote
