★What are the best Twitter clients?
Tweetdeck for Mac - multiple account support is great bringing Twitter, Facebook and  into a single application. Puts all your feeds front and center. Favorite feature - tweet popups while app is hidden behind other windows. The new Tweetdeck for iPhone released today total redesign, much cleaner, and can reduce  swiping through columns - Combine multiple feeds into a single column while retaining earlier column capability for individual feeds when needed, easily access friends bio, feeds and favorites. Flipboard for iPad - the easiest way to seamlessly read tweets with linked content and photos an excellent user experience.
