★Mobile Applications: What's the most innovative app?(NB: mostly for Android here)
Tasker. What is Tasker ? It is an automation app. You can automate almost everything on your phone. Want to turn off internet on your phone during night ? Want to send SMS automatically to someone who called you during a meeting saying that you are busy ? Want to start music whenever any headset is plugged in  ? List of actions you can perform. It comes at 200 Rs. (3.25 USD). Can't afford it ? Don't worry, there's a free alternative called Llama. Do you know what's the icing on cake ? It does not require internet.
