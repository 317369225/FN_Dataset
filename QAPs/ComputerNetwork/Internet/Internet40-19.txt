IoT is all about connecting everything. in order to do that, you need everything to talk the same language. This is the greatest challenge of IoT adoption. and whoever conquers this will take the lead on this wave. Currently there are many vendors setting their eyes on this holy grail, but the key for the "language of everything" is in fact in open standard adopted by and supported by a wide community. There are quite a few initiatives for standardizing IoT on different levels, backed by different industry players such as Google, Samsung and Microsoft.
