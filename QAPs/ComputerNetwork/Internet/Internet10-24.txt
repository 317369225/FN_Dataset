★Is the Internet spoiling young children?
There's some preliminary research that reading on a laptop screen is different from reading behavior with paper - even with the same article printed out. Referred to as the "F - pattern", it seems that, on the screen, we are likely to read less and less of the content as we scroll down.   F shaped reading pattern and the digital native | Education   If this in fact is the case, then, it would have an impact on the reading habits of young children.
