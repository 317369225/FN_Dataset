★Is the Internet killing religion?
Three primary factors why millennials are leaving: Cultural Christianity of the 80s and particularly the 90s.  This culminates in a thin version of faith and an ability to withstand faith-trials that life sends you. Being more open about latent dissatisfaction and frustration. More media influence across the board advocating for a culture of materialism and excess.
