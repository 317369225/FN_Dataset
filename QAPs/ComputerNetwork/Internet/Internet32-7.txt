★Is the Internet killing religion?
It really only takes a small amount of attention to international news to realize what a danger religion is. In that the internet has become a principal news source, it is a vehicle for this enlightenment - but given that the internet is also a source of broadcasting religious propaganda of all sorts, it is clear that the internet is only a vehicle, not a cause.  It is  what is being done in the name of religion that is the cause .
