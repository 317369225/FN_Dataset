★What are the effects of wireless communication?
In the long term, it's like driving a car all the time, instead of walking or running. It's faster, more comfortable, but it'll get you fat, physical tasks will become more and more difficult. Same with all the technology, people are getting used to looking at a screen and texting instead of looking at the person next to them and talking. The more time is spent communicating virtually, the harder it becomes to have a real conversation.    Embed Quote
