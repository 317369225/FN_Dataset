The Tankless Wall Mount Dual-Flush Toilet The tank is embedded into the wall, saving space and providing for a more elegant, minimalistic look. The bowl is wall mounted, making the floor underneath very easy to clean. The dual flush means that the toilet has two modes: One to flush liquid (that only uses half the water in the tank) and the other to flush solids, saving a significant amount of water.
