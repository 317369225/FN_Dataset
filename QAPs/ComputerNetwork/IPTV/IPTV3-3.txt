★What is the difference between VoD and iPTV?
IPTV is a general term which means delivering television services over the Internet. One can compare it to satellite television services. VoD is one such service that IPTV provides. Broadly IPTV provides following services: 1. Linear TV - Plain live television programming. 2. Time-Shifted TV: Viewers can watch older television programs on demand. Few services can also remove advertisements from them. (Airtel's IPTV in India became very successful because of this feature). 3. VoD: Video on demand, this has nothing to do with television programming, but is a catalogue  based service to let user watch a movies when they choose too. VoD is similar to on demand video service provided by many satellite TV providers.
