Rise of Nations was later launched by Microsoft Studios bringing advanced features like missiles and space exploration in the original AOE game. And then there is Counter-Strike, which requires skill in mouse control for first person shooting. This game became popular among students as it provided a platform to divert their study rage into gaming.
