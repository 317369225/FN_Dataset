★What are some of the peer-to-peer consumer startups?This is a follow-up to a TechCrunch article "The P2P Evolution" to try and crowdsource all P2P startups: tcrn.ch/lV9Hiz
Neighborrow.com - sharing items keywifi.com - sharing wifi guestvessel.com - sharing apts. meetingwave.com - sharing a coffee or lunch or other meeting activity airbnb.com - renting apts I know there are lots of others.  I'm a big fan since reduces waste (both time and things).
