★What is peer-to-peer lending?
An example of P2P company would be new era lending. It is a private peer-to-peer lender that provides short-term 3 to 12 months commercial loans for a range of operational needs. Typically,  clients or 'unrelated individuals' choose to finance temporary dips in cash flow, invest in new equipment, or make time sensitive inventory purchases. They provide funding for small and medium businesses in all industries with at least 6 months operating history and of minimum $15K in revenue per month. As a private lender, New Era Lending invests in projects that institutional banks may deny or simply might take months to complete.    Embed Quote
