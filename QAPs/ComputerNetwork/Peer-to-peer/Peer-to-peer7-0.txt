★Is the peer-to-peer lending category worth investing in?
If you believe that P2P will supercede traditional money lending channels, then it should be a very valuable investment category. Traditional money lending is probably a trillion-dollar marketplace and capturing any significant portion of that spending would be lucrative for new businesses that can effectively capitalize on the shift. However, the difficulty with investments is often not in identifying the secular trend but finding a specific vehicle that will capitalize the opportunity while minimizing investment risks.    Embed Quote
