★Why do programmers love working at night?
Let me break it down, If you are a hobbyist programer, that is, you try out projects of your own, the only time left is the night time. Also, as all of you told 'fewer distractions'. Mum is not around to give you chores, and friends wouldn't want to hangout at ungodly hours. At the office most usable daytime goes off in coffee break, lunch, coffee break +++. If you are not on one of those breaks, you are either at "scrum" or "feature meetings" where we do assessment, impact, etc. Most Importantly:  fewer living things to interact with= more work done And Download Speeds
