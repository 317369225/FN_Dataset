★How should a person negotiate for the purchase of a domain name that is not parked?
Buying an existing website domain name is like buying a pre-owned home or commercial building but unlike with real estate, the reputation of the previous website domain name owner comes with the purchase.  If they've been blacklisted as a spammer, then you inherit that reputation. As evidence by the other expert answers here, there are a lot of risks and cautions to know about before buying an existing website address.  It's somewhat like the wild, wild west with lots of speculators who purchased website domain names who want to get rich selling them.  Buyer beware!
