Running a Tor hidden service requires that you host a web server on your domain, and point your running installation of Tor to that open port. Your address becomes hidden within the obfuscation of the Tor service, and no money every exchanges hands. Running a Tor hidden service does not require Namecoin. Namecoin is a distributed bitcoin-like system for reserving domains in the .bit namespace. Instructions for running a Tor hidden service can be found here: https://www.torproject.org/docs/...
