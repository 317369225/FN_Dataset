★What are some books with interconnected short stories?Something along the same lines as Cloud Atlas. It could be any genre. The connection between the stories should be fascinating.
The main one I could think of is Robert Heinlein's Assignment In Eternity. It's mainly two short novels: Gulf and Lost Legacy. In Gulf, "the greatest superspy of them all is revealed as the leader of a league of supermen and women who can't quite decide what to do with the rest of us..." In Lost Legacy, "it is proved that we are all members of that league - or would be, if we but had eyes to see..."    Embed Quote
