★Are high blood pressure and diabetes always interconnected?
A recent study: Blood Pressure Lowering Treatments Benefit Type II Diabetes Patients, concluded that type II diabetics with high blood pressure benefited from bp-lowering treatment earlier than people who don't suffer from diabetes. For some diseases diabetes sufferers are at a higher risk for, like stroke, there was a significant benefit of blood pressure treatment lowering the bp under 130mm Hg systolic.    Embed Quote
