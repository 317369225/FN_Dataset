Companies, that sustain, will offer services that customers are willing to pay for delivered by a efficient model that allow them to make a profit. In wireless and mobile space most customers are accepting to pay a premium compared to fixed wired access. Delivering a bit over wireless is in almost all cases more expensive than in a wireline case. Few companies have found a successful lasting business model offering something unlimited for a low price. Only in cases of being a new market entrant this has been seen as a viable entry strategy.
