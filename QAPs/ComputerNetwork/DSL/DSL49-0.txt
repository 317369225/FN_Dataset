★Broadband: What is faster. Cable or phone line via DSL?
Well traditionally cable has had higher theortical limits, but these change as technology improves.  Other factors come into play.  Distance from CO is a limiting factor for DSL.  Cable is limited based on how it is carved up between the subscribers and which DOCSIS version is being used.  As it stands today, cable is providing more bandwidth for the buck.
