★Can you use a VPN on an iPhone or iPad?
As long as you connect through the following protocols or authentication methods, it's been supported by all IOS devices include iPhone and iPad: L2TP/IPSec with user authentication by MS-CHAPV2 Password, RSA SecurID or CryptoCard, and machine authentication by shared secret. PPTP with user authentication by MS-CHAPV2 Password, RSA SecurID, or CRYPTOCard. Cisco IPSec with user authentication by Password, RSA SecurID, or CRYPTOCard, and machine authentication by shared secret and certificates. Cisco IPSec supports VPN On Demand for domains you specify during device configuration.1 Juniper Junos Pulse and Cisco AnyConnect, using the appropriate VPN app from the App Store.
