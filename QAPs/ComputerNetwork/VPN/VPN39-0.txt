★What is the advantage in VPN(?
Depends on your use case. Businesses use VPN for: Allowing remote workers, branch offices, partners, distributors etc connect to the DC to access critical business applications securely. In newer use cases, they connect different cloud providers forming a single secure private network.Many vendors such as Cisco Systems, Citrix, Juniper Networks, InstaSafe, Pertino work on this use cases. Individuals use VPN to secure their Internet connection to maintain anonymity while browsing. There are lot of companies working on this model too. In summary, VPNs are advantageous when one would like to secure network accesses.    Embed Quote
