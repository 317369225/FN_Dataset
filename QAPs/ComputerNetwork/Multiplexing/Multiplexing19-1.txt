★What cinemas or multiplexes in Kolkata offer couples seating or couch seating arrangement?(no arm rest between two seats)?
All Gold Class tickets in Kolkata multiplexes include an option for couple-seating couches. A peek at the seating arrangement in sites such as BookMyShow will show you that such seats are generally available at the back of the theater. The multiplexes which offer such seating are: Mani Square, Fame, Broadway and PVR Cinemas.    Embed Quote
