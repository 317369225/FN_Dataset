★How can I extend the range of my wireless router that doesn't have antennas?
If you don't have external antennas on your wifi router, you have limited options. Extend range by looking at your firmware, some firmware will allow you to increase the output power of your wifi router. Better placement of your wifi router device. If it is tucked away in the corner in your study, you may want to invest a little bit of money in an Ethernet cable and have it connected and placed some where central in your house/office. Reflective technology, you can place aluminum foil walls around the router to help better reflection (this is a very easy hack). Invest in Wifi extender.    Embed Quote
