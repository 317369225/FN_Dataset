★How can i connect to bsnl broadband internet with belkin basic N150 wireless router?
Connections 1.  Connect the telephone line to your ADSL Modem.  (Belkin N150 is only a router) 2.  Get the LAN (RJ45) output from the modem and input to your router. 3.  Connect your computer to the router via wifi/lan. Settings Modem 1.  Set your PPPoE values -> BSNL User ID, Password etc. (Configure your Belkin N150 router for BSNL broadband) 2. Ensure your modem is connecting to the ISP.  Check the summary tab.
