★How do I set up a wireless network for conference on a budget?
A per-user bandwidth controlled Social WIFI router could be used as a building block. Multiple of these units will be required to support areas where you want coverage. Social Authentication and sharing also permits you to build relationships for future communication. a Verizon 4g LTE USB modem can be used with the router in case you do not have (or don't want to use) event venue's Internet connection.
