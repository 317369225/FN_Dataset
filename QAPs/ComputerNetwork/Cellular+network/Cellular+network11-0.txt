Verizon Wireless has the widest coverage nationwide on its own network, based on the number of mobile-switching centers where it's colocated to backhaul cellular traffic. Its average bandwidth is also the highest nationwide, based on Net Index Explorer by Ookla and Fastest Mobile Networks 2014.
