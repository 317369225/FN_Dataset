★What are the hottest research topics in network security?
One is big data analytics. We log everything - from servers, browsers, firewalls, proxies, IPS devices and many more. We get daily external feeds like vulnerability disclosures and even social media intelligence. For a large multinational, that's several hundred Gigs a day. A lot of money is being spent on solving the perennial needle in a haystack problem: How to we detect, rank and alert on the tiny threads of data that may indicate security breaches? Can we do it in real time in the Security Operations Centre and what are the most efficient triage and response processes?    Embed Quote
