Programming = thinking^2 Like any creative medium, it gives you the framework – and more important, the courage – to turn the stuff in your head into stuff you can actually see and use. And then, to remake that stuff, as it re-influences your thinking. Programming helps you structure positive mental feedback loops. It lets you have a productive, organized dialectic in your own noggin.
