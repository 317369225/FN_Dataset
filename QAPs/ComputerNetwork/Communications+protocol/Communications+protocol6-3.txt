★Is there any standard communication protocol for Internet of Things (IoT)? Is any group working on that?
There are plenty of Internet of Things standards, protocols, frameworks:  - [AllJoyn][1] by AllSeen Alliance  - [AMQP][2] by OASIS  - [CoAP][3] by IP for Smart Objects Alliance  - [DDS][4] by Object Management Group  - [HTTP][5] by W3C  - IoT Platform by Intel  - [MQTT][6] by IBM  - [Open Interconnect Consortium][7]  - [Stomp][8] by Stomp Spec Group  - [Thread][9] by Thread Group  - [WAMP][10] by Tavendo  - [XMPP][11] by XMPP Standards Foundation  - [ZigBee][12] by ZigBee Alliance  - [Z-Wave][13] by Z-Wave Alliance   [1]: https://www.alljoyn.org/   [2]: http://amqp.org/   [3]: https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc7252   [4]: http://portals.omg.org/dds/   [5]: http://www.w3.org/   [6]: http://mqtt.org/   [7]: http://www.openinterconnect.org/   [8]: http://stomp.github.io/   [9]: http://www.threadgroup.org/   [10]: http://wamp.ws/   [11]: http://xmpp.org/   [12]: http://www.zigbee.org/   [13]: http://www.z-wave.com/
