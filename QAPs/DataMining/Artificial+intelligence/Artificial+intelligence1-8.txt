★What will be the next big thing in artificial intelligence in the next 10 to 20 years?
According to the McKinsey Global Institute (MGI), the business and economics research arm of McKinsey & Company, advances in Automation of knowledge work, based on machine learning and natural user interfaces (e.g., voice recognition) that make it possible to automate many knowledge worker tasks that have long been regarded as impossible or impractical for machines to perform, are the second most important technological innovation today. Knowledge worker employment costs represent 27% of global employment costs.    Embed Quote
