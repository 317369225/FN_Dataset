Will artificial intelligence render human music makers as useless?
Yes. But it will take a long time. Just like the Spinning jenny is more reliable and cheaper and hence rendered human spinners (is that the name) useless, A.I.s will do so in the long run for human cognitive work. However, I doubt that this will happen in my lifetime. Emily Howell is the name of a computer program written by University of California, Santa Cruz professor David Cope.
