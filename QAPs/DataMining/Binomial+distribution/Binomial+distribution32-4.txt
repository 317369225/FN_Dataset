A guy walks in to a bar with an alligator under his arm. "I'll open this alligator's mouth and place my genitals inside!", he yells out to everyone.  "The gator will close his mouth for one minute, then open it at my command.  If my genitals emerge unscathed, everyone here buys me a drink!" Everyone in the bar agrees. The guy drops his pants and puts his genitals in the alligator's mouth. The alligator closes its mouth. After a minute, the guy takes a beer bottle off the bar,  bangs the gator on the top of its head with the bottle, and the gator opens up its mouth.  The man removes his genitals, entirely unscathed. Everyone buys him drinks. "I'll pay anyone who's willing to give it a try $100", the man then says.   After a while, a hand goes up in the back of the bar. It's a blonde woman. "I'll do it," she says. "Just don't hit me on the head with the bottle."    Embed Quote
