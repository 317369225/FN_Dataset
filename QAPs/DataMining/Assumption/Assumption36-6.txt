Is it a common assumption that an atheist is immoral and depraved?
The asked question is: "Is it a common assumption that an Atheist is immoral and depraved?" After ~4 decades as a publicly active adult atheist it is indeed still my experience that it is generally a common assumption among most religionists (especially among fundamentalists preachers for whom it seems to be a fundamental certainty) that we atheists are with rare exception an untrustworthy pack of moral-compassless depravites who only by accident act ethically except when we think somebody is watching. And yet many of those religionists who think that of we atheists also think it is OK (yea, a duty) to slaughter children if [alleged] God tells them to do so!  Go figure...    Embed Quote
