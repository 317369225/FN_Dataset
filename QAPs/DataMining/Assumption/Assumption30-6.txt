What are some examples of stupid assumptions that intelligent people often make?
Assuming a college degree makes one smarter. Assuming age equals expertise. Assuming you must be correct since everyone else agrees with you. Assuming there is a good answer to a decision. Assuming someone else is right or wrong, so you must be wrong or right. Assuming there are only X possible outcomes to a problem. Assuming that if you are wrong there are no consequences. Assuming intelligent people make better decisions. In summation, making most any kind of assumption requires leaping past many obvious conditions, and some not so obvious. Making assumptions is generally not the best way to come to an accurate conclusion. At least that is my assumption...    Embed Quote
