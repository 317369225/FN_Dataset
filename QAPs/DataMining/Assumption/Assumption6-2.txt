Does mathematics really exist in the universe or is it just a human assumption?
Humans are incapable of looking at things in the world and not imposing patterns on what they see.  Are the patterns just in their heads or do they correspond to something that really and truly exists.  Even such a worthy physicist as Eugene Wigner punted on this question.
