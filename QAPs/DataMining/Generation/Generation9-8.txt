★Did the Baby Boomer generation commit generational theft?
The fact that America is kind of broken right now has little to do with the voting behavior of the Boomers and a lot to do with the general belief that what is good for businesses is always good for everybody. If you want to blame the prevalence of this belief on the Boomers, fine, but doing so is misguided as blind faith in Capitalism Uber All was deeply entrenched  in the 20th Century American Psyche across the age spectrum, from the wood-paneled board rooms of World War II era business tycoons to the cannabis-scented bull sessions of Generation X Libertarians.    Embed Quote
