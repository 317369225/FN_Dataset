★Why is the younger generation so opposed to capitalism?
There's a saying in Germany: 'Wer mit Zwanzig kein Sozialist ist, hat kein Herz. Wer mit Vierzig Sozialist ist, hat kein Hirn.' It roughly translates to 'One who isn't a socialist at twenty has no heart. One who is a socialist at forty has no brain'. While I don't quite agree with the sentiment in its bold simplicity, there is some truth in there...    Embed Quote
