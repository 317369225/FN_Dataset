★What is the best way to store time series data in Redis?
Gemcutter does this for daily download stats, like on: http://rubygems.org/profiles/qrush There's basically a hash per day for each gem and version, the keys/values look like... 2011-01-20 => 1983 2011-01-21 => 2999 etc. A daily cron job is in place to roll over today's stats (in a sorted set) into the historical hashes.
