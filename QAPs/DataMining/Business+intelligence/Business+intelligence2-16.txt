Which business intelligence tools have the best user interface?
Tableau is by far the best BI tool I have ever used. Apart from the great web interface and ease-of-use, it lets you directly connect to many big data interfaces. Tableau's JavaScript API allows your visualization/dashboard to be embedded in Web sites. The recent release includes R integration which helps predictive analytics capabilities. They keep evolving for current technology needs and trends.    Embed Quote Updated 6 Feb, 2014. 891 views.
