What are the most cost effective Business Intelligence tools?
"Cost effective" is a fairly fluid requirement.  Clearly you don't spend a dollar to save  or generate a  dollar, so you would likely want to tailor the solution and associated BI investment to your expected ROI.  If you're tackling a $10m problem, your BI cost threshold is clearly higher than if you're tacking a $10k problem! That being said, I would suggest that before you start looking to evaluate tools (and costs), you clearly articulate your objectives and requirements.  From there you will be in a better position to evaluate tools and BI delivery options. For example, data integration requirements,  on-premise vs.SaaS; hybrid-delivery; reporting and dashboarding requirements functional and technical resource requirements et al...    Embed Quote
