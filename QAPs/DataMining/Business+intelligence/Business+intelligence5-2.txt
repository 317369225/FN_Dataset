Make or buy BI (Business Intelligence)?
Most online and mobile firms shouldn't build their own, the cost is too prohibitive and it shifts attention away from their core competence. This is especially true for non-enterprise companies. CoolaData has a great new blog post about this, detailing the costs of making -- which is in the hundreds of thousands of dollars, pre-maintenance. Here's the post The True Cost of Building a Big Data Solution - CoolaData. It breaks them down into three main categories: infrastructure, software and developers.
