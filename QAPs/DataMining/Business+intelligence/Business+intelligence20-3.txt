Are small businesses interested in Business Intelligence (BI) software?
Small businesses are interested in the information that you can get from Business Intelligence software platforms.  However, as Quora User pointed out small business typically do not have the resources or skills to fully utilize some of the existing platforms.  There are some interesting cloud Business Intelligence solutions that are up and coming that could serve the small business market needs.  Potentially even more important though is a way to get acces to this information while on the go. Business has become mobile and decisions cannot always wait until you have access to your computer or are in the office.  As a result, having some of your most important Business Intelligence information at your finger tips is empowering for any business regardless of size.    Embed Quote
