Business Intelligence: What are some good BI tools for MySQL?
Jolicharts might be a good BI solution that connects to MySQL if you are looking for cost-efficient, easy-to-use solution that can be deployed very fast for a high number of users. Jolicharts is a very new application that was entirely built for being integrating into SaaS applications and allows to manage both visualizations and data (even big amounts of data). All charts and dashboards can easily be embedded and their content can be managed through a quite powerful API. Jolicharts comes with most of the things typical BI solutions do: - Custom queries - Integrated data warehousing - In-memory caching - Multiple datasources (csv, xls, databases,...) - Filters - Multi-sheet dashboards - Unlimited users - etc.    Embed Quote
