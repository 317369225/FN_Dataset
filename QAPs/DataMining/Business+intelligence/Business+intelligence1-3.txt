What is the difference between business intelligence and business analytics?
I hope it's not too late to answer. Business Analytics is the broad analysis techniques such as statistics, economics, operations research and computer science. Business Intelligence, at its inception, it's the way to transform data into meaningful information. Data Mining, the technique used to analyze hidden patterns in large data set. One of the first data mining project in the world is when retailer (Mark & Spencers in UK) wanted to analyze customer transaction data.
