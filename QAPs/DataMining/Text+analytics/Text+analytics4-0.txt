★What are the next big trends in text analytics?
Probably the biggest trend in text analytics is trying to crack sarcasm. It has always been a limitation when dealing with unstructured text and it really hit the news big this past summer when the secret service of the United States announced that they were looking for a text analytics tool that could decode sarcasm. The problem with sarcasm is that humans can't even interpret sarcasm with 100% accuracy.
