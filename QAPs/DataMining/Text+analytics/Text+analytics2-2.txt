I would describe the main value for text analytics is to make search engines smarter (create meta data for texts). If you use Aarkstore - Global Text Analytics Market APIs to automate things like entity tagging and Sentiment Analysis you can create new dimensions of search. This is valuable both for normal users and companies that now can make searches like: Give me the most positive texts for the company Ford and compare it to Give me the most positive texts for the person Ford    Embed Quote
