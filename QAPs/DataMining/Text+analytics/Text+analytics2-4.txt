★What are the new commercial applications for text analytics?What  has been done commercially that is recent?
Most data being created today is unstructured. IBM stated 90% of all the world's data has been created in the past 24 months. This makes text analytics an incredibly important and powerful tool. A popular commercial application is social sentiment analysis. Markets have always been emotional. Customers used to have discussions around the water cooler. But today these conversations are taking place online. Text analytics is being leveraged heavily for market research and consumer insights extraction from unstructured data online.    Embed Quote
