What are the hot and/or important topics in cloud data management and cloud computing research?

Cloud management has to: · continue to make cloud easier to use; ·  provide security policies for the cloud environment; ·  allow safe cloud operations and ease migrations; ·  provide for financial controls and tracking; ·  audit and reporting for compliance.
