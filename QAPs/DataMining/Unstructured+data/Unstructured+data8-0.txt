★What are the obstacles to solving the unstructured data extraction problem?
Large diversity of specific tasks that are to be solved on unstructured data corpora. Search and entity extraction are somewhat solved problems. Knowledge inference is a tricky part, but fundamental research is very academic these days while corporations prefer to set local goals and hit those instead. As of today, for virtually any real-world problem the winning solution is not the one that has solved it "right", but the one that presented the best product with the right UI, that is leveraging existing, established techniques.    Embed Quote
