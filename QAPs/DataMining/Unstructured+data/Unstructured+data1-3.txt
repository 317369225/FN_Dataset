★What is the difference between structured and unstructured data?
“Gartner defines unstructured data as content that does not conform to a specific, pre-defined data model,” writes Gartner’s Darin Stewart. “It tends to be human-generated, and people-oriented content that does not fit neatly into database tables.” Structured Big Data is information drawn from connected sensors, web logs, financial applications, and point of sale systems. As every process and piece of equipment within an organization becomes more connected, the strain placed by information flow becomes too much for old database software to handle.
