★Self-Improvement: How do I manage my time and increase my knowledge-base?
The best way will be to watch videos on youtube on your area of intrest, if you are having issues of time. Try to prioritize things in your daily schedule. Give time to things that are really important and reduce the distractions that you have in your daily life. If possible get up early in the morning, plan your day and read something from self help section for an hour or so. Then work out for sometime and then go for your studies. Try to utilize every moment that you get for learning something new of your field. The best will be to make a habit of reading one technical article of your field daily. Slowly you will be able to create the balance that you want.    Embed Quote
