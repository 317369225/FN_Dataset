★What is the legality of web scraping?
It is legal to scrape factual data. For eg: You can read about this in the Fiest vs. Rural case: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fei... Here are the salient points: It is a long-standing principle of United States copyright law that "information" is not copyright-able. In regard to collections of facts, Justice O'Connor states that copyright can only apply to the creative aspects of collection: the creative choice of what data to include or exclude, the order and style in which the information is presented, etc., but not on the information itself.    Embed Quote
