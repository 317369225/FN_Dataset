★What are some recommended content marketing strategies for beginners?
Before you start talking media, delivery vehicles, or even topics, get a rock-solid understanding of the audience you're trying to reach. Go beyond demographics and really understand what makes them tick: What are their interests? What do they value? What are their hopes and dreams for the future? What challenges keep them up at night? Once you've created an accurate persona representing your target audience, most other answers will naturally fall into place.    Embed Quote
