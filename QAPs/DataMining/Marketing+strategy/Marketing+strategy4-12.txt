★How important is content in a digital marketing strategy?
In a single word: EXTREMELY! The simple answer is that digital has changed the marketing landscape forever because it has shifted the balance of power between seller and buyer Before digital, marketers could just bark their messages at their target audiences, promoting their brand in a one-way conversation. In the digital world, consumers are empowered. They have endless choices. They can voice their opinions. They can look to influencers and friends to shape their opinions about your brands. They are always one click away from being engaged by your competitors. So where does content fit into all of this? Your target audiences are looking to be entertained, informed and enlightened. They want to be associated with brands that not only “get” them, but cater to them. Content marketing is all about “loving” your audiences and earning their love in return.    Embed Quote
