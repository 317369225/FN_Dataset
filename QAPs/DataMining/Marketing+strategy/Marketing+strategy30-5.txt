★What is a good content marketing strategy for a digital agency?
Further to the answers already given, CONTENT CURATION is great way to inexpensively and effectively compliment your content marketing strategy for your client. An example of a company that uses content curation well is Slimfast (see: http://magazine.slimfast.co.uk/). Their site publishes up to 15 new articles a day, of which 80% are curated for free from quality bloggers who are already writing content on topics such as 'healthy eating' and 'exercise'.
