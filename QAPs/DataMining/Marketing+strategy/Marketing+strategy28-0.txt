★How should I begin research on a content marketing strategy?
Try consulting Google's own search volume statistics and trending tools in order to more closely align with topics that people are actually searching for. While Google has made a point of punishing content firms that appear to put out low-quality content based on search trends, there's nothing wrong with making sure that there is evidence that a market exists for high-quality content that you'd like to offer.
