★What are the best inbound marketing strategies?
The question does appear a bit broad but I think the answer is still straight forward. The best way to increase your sales pipeline is to create a strategy  that mirrors your sales process. Your content should nurture the buyer  down the sales funnel while providing them with useful content so that  by the time they reach the point of sale, they are much more sales  ready. You have now armed your customers with many more pieces of  information that will influence their buying decision.
