Start with organic search and slowly apply dollars across the above spectrum. Do not move onto the next category until you have mastered the current one. For instance, do not enter the affiliate market until you have a firm grasp on paid search. This is the path we followed when we rolled out GoToMyPC. We were one of the top five advertisers on the Internet (as measured in dollars spent) for nearly two years. We were able to spend large amounts of money because we were highly metrics driven. For every dollar we spent, we knew we were going to get a couple bucks in return.
