★What is the best marketing strategy?
Has a unique value proposition Clear aims and objectives Has evidence to show there is customer demand or a viable way to generate demand Shows there is a way to displace or out innovat marketplace competition Explains where the business sits in the current market compared to competitors Has an engine of growth that creates compound market advantages for the business as effort is applied Has a clear calculation of the time, talent and cash required to achieve the goals Clearly highlights the near term big picture challenges Illustrates the leap of faith assumptions that must still be tested. E.g. "Customers like mustard therefore they will like mustard on potato chips". Illustrates the tactical response to the near term marketing challenges Using marketing technology is not strategic marketing.  Tactics enable marketing to happen. Marketing strategy comes from diagnosing the near term 'go to market' challenges and seeking to overcome them.    Embed Quote
