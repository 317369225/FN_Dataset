★What is Facebook's marketing strategy?High quality strategy, niche strategy, etc.
Word of mouth, the network effect and FOMO. The reason people join Facebook is psychological. Your friends are there. You want to interact with them and be in touch. More importantly, you have a Fear Of Missing Out (FOMO): if you are not on Facebook and all your friends are there and chatting about cool stuff (and about you) you want to be there.    Embed Quote
