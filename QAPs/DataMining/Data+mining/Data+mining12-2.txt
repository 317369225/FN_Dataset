What is data mining?
Data mining is a process of extracting specific information from large databases and presenting relevant and usable information that can be used to solve business problems. There are different kinds of services in this process like text mining, web mining, audio and video mining, pictorial data mining and social network data mining. It is done through simple or highly specific software. By outsourcing data mining all the work can be done faster and with reduced operational cost. Also, specialized companies can to use new technologies to gather information that are otherwise impossible to locate manually.
