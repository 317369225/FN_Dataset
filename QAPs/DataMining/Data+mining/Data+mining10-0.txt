Why is data mining used?
Here's some interesting numbers which will set the perspective right. From human civilization to 2010, the amount of data was created was 10% and from 2010 to 2014, 90% of data was created (Source: IBM Research). With rapid growth in data generation, so many signals that can lead to actionable insights may be missed out. For this reason, it becomes important to use data mining. The above point was a brief abstract. There could be tons of other reasons. Broadly speaking, data mining is used to because of massive amount of data that needs to be processed.    Embed Quote
