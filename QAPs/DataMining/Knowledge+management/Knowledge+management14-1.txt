★What are the best practices in knowledge management for IT Service management (ITSM)?
Knowledge Centered Support is a best practices and the methodology is complimentary to ITSM. Think of KCS as how to structure your incident record and KE database: plus the workflow needed to allow you to find the KE record easily. It is the methodology that works. KCS is really a collection of best practices for Knowledge Management - from Structured Problem Solving, to a metrics framework, to leadership - all focused around the capture, structure and reuse of information/knowledge.    Embed Quote
