★What is the use of knowledge management?
Knowledge Management is a method by which an organization can have a record of all the available expertise and training within the organization. It can help with creating project teams, meeting regulatory requirements, identify needs for further training and development, identify hiring and promotion criteria, populate a location where others can locate colleagues with specific knowledge for advice. It can also point out potential weaknesses in an organization that retirement, resignations and promotions may create. That is a summary only. Effective Knowledge Management is an important concept for any organization. Sometimes in larger organizations the leadership teams are not aware of the expertise or lack of within the organization within a pertinent time frame.    Embed Quote
