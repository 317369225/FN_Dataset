★What is knowledge management?
In the simplest form, KM is knowing what you or your organization knows. This is a simple concept with an extraordinarily complex execution. There are a wide range of activities within KM to record, store and make available  knowledge. Most practitioners come at KM from their own perspective: IT focuses on storage, retrieval and search; HR focuses on people and interactions and Library Science focuses on classification and organization. Successful KM projects require all of these perspectives.
