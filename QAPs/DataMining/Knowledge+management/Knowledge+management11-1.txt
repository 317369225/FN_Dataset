★What is the best way to achieve Knowledge Management in the workplace?How to tap and share institutional knowledge?
The glib answer is JFDI. A more serious answer is to start small with a well identified scope. Codify the who / what / where / how*. Measure your success, adjust and expand the scope. * - more details Who will be involved? (Who's your champion? Who are the core? Who is the audience?) What will they share? ("Is it okay to post bug reports here?") Where will they share it? (On a wiki? through a federation of blogs? on facebook?) How will they share it? (i.e. what are you standards. straight chat logs or Quora like expositions)    Embed Quote
