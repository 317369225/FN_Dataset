★What is knowledge management?
Knowledge management can be  defined in simpler terms as the managing of knowledge, it is exactly what it says. Knowledge management as been regarded as a form of resource for an organisation and any given resource needs to be managed. Thus making knowledge management a technique or process used in an organisation to control the creation of knowledge, as well as the process of identifiying and spreading lessons learned.     http://www.km-forum.org/what_is.htm    Embed Quote
