★What are the knowledge management startups?
This is a hard question answer thoroughly. The topic itself ranges from enterprise content management to productivity and collaboration tools. Some larger players that are gaining traction with large enterprises are Slack and Box. Both are challenging incumbent systems, like SharePoint. A noteworthy early stage company is Collected, which is carving out a niche in contextually aware software. It flips the content search equation on its head by using context within your current document to suggest you usable snippets of content from your organization's knowledge libraries.    Embed Quote
