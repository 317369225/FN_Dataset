★What are the top 5 barriers to Knowledge Management?
Doing it to lower your costs. They may go down, but your motivation should be to provide better faster answers to customers. Not getting IT, or whoever owns your infrastructure to buy into KM. Employees thinking it replaces them. Forcing customers to use a poorly implemented KM solution when they have better support experiences following other support paths Making the creation of knowledge separate from doing your normal job.
