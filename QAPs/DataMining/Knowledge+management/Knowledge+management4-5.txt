★What are the top 5 barriers to Knowledge Management?
There is a huge amount of risk involved in publishing knowledge/information without due diligence and some sort of controls in place. I believe this is too often overlooked or de-prioritized, but it is the control of the content that will make or break any knowledge system’s overall success.   Too often we forget that there is a “Management” component to “Knowledge Management” that requires the involvement and dedication of people to make the system and processes function as designed.    Embed Quote
