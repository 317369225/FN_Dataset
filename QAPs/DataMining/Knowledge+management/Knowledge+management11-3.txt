Things to take note before you implement KM: 1. Conduct KM Readiness Assessment-to know how ready is your organization 2. Define your KM Model, Framework and Methodology-without this you lost your battle without a fight 3. Identify Champion-establish KM Team / CoP  (depends on your implementation model) 4. Establish Strategic Plan-define direction of KM, set the objectives, success factor, output and desired impact 5. Proper Execution-Manage KM project with proper 'project management' method 6.
