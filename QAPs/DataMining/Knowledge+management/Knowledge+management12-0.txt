★Who hates enterprise knowledge management?
I  haven't used a capability management software per se, the closest thing to knowledge management software that I have used would be project management and document management and collaboration, mostly on the Microsoft Platform. I have also worked with tools from other vendors. I believe there is always something to hate about enterprise software. These systems take months and sometime years to deploy and they are designed to make work boring, hence they are revered by accountants and traditional functions within organizations. I believe the space is ripe for innovation through simpler and more intuitive tools coupled with SaaS delivery model that are designed to cut down implementation and training cycles. This applies virtually to all sorts of enterprise solutions including knowledge management which is notoriously known for being too hyped a field    Embed Quote
