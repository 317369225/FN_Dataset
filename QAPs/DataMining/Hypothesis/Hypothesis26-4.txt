★What is a null hypothesis in layman's terms?
The null hypothesis wants to prove that your statement is true by mere chance. But it can't. It can only show that its possible that its chance. When is rejected, its good. Because you can say: chance alone is not a good argument why my sample has this property. This event marks the beginning of the journey to discover new relationships. But be aware that even if is acceptable, this means not that chance is really the cause of the observation. You might try again.
