★What is the Gaia hypothesis?How much interest does it currently generate in the scientific community?
Gaia hypothesis is the belief that the Earth acts like a living organism and it has a soul. Gaia is actually the soul of the Earth. Although one may argue that the level of connection between the countless species and phenomena on our planet are enough to make it a monolithic organism, there is little to no interest of the scientific community towards this subject, basically because there is no evidence.    Embed Quote
