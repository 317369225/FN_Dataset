★How will search engine optimization change with the advent of the semantic web?How will SEO be affected/changed with Semantic Web gaining more prominence.
Many of the less knowledgeable practices, outsourced link building, gratuitous formatting, keyword stuffing and such, will no longer work. Businesses will be best served by creating a search strategy that starts with creating a Core Metadata, that is a list of term phrases for which they want to appear prominently and that are represented by the content on the site intersected with a list of terms that represent how customers actually look for those products or services. This Core Metadata then becomes the framework for a content strategy and link strategy that supports the search engine's perception of "aboutness" within the confines of their many algorithms.    Embed Quote
