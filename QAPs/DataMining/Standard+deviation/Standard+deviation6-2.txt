★What is the sum of average and standard deviation?
Here is a detailed example for your doubt., Consider a set X of numbers 5,10,15,20,25                             Step 1 :         Mean = Sum of X values / N(Number of values)                  = (5+10+15+20+25) / 5                  = 75 / 5                  = 15                                                  Step 2 :          To find the variance,             Subtract the mean from each of the values,             5-15 = -10             10-15 = -5             15-15 = 0             20-15 = 5             25-15 = 10 Now square all the answers you have got from subtraction.             (-10)2 = 100             (-5)2   = 25             (0)2     = 0             (5)2    = 25             (10)2 = 100                          Add all the Squared numbers,             100 + 25 + 0 + 25 + 100 = 250                          Divide the sum of squares by (n-1)             250 / (5-1) = 250 / 4 = 62.5                          Hence Variance =  62.5 Source: Standard Deviation Calculator    Embed Quote
