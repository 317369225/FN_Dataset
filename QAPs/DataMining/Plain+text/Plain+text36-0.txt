★What is the best way to punctuate around URLs in plain text documents?
The Chicago Manual of Style 16th section 14.11 and 14.12 edition suggests that no punctuation should be used for URLs. in context.  They used to suggest angle brackets, but have deprecated their use. There are lots specific details about handling line breaks or situations that call for quotation marks, but in general they should include the protocol portion so that they can be cut and pasted into an address bar to reach the site.
