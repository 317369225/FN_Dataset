Format-preserving encryption (FPE) schemes in cryptography is a category of encryption algorithms that encrypt in a way that the output (ciphertext) is in the same format as the input (plaintext). In this case you want to ciphertext to be an English dictionary word. This academic paper discusses such algorithms and their security in details.    Embed Quote
