★What are the parsing techniques used by modern compilers?Do they use parser generators (if yes, which) ? Which kind of parser algorithms do they use (and why) ?
They use either a hand-written recursive-descent parser, or a LALR(1) parser generator. Examples: GCC does not use parser generators. GCC C parser, gcc/c-parser.c, is a single-file hand-written recursive-descent parser in C. GCC C++ parser, gcc/cp/parser.c, is a single-file hand-written recursive-descent parser in C. Clang does not use parser generators. Clang C language family parser, lib/Parse, is a multiple-file hand-written recursive-descent parser in C++. OCaml parser, parsing/parser.mly, uses ocamlyacc, a LALR(1) parser generator generating OCaml. GHC Haskell parser, compiler/parser/Parser.y.pp, uses Happy, a LALR(1) parser generator generating Haskell.    Embed Quote
