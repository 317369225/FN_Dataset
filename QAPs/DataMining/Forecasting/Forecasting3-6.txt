★What is the difference between budgeting and forecasting?In a large organization who calculates each and how? I look at the monthly reports sent out by the company and the budget and forecast never match. Why?
Put simply;   Budgeting is a quantified financial plan and is done to allow; -      Revenue and expenditure forecasts to be detailed -      Allows the forecast to be measured -      Provide a cost restriction   Forecasting is a statement that predicts or estimates the outcome of an event or project.    Embed Quote
