★What are the best market research techniques?
That's one of those "it depends" answers. It depends what kind of market research you need to do. If you're at the very initial stages of figuring out your product and your market you will probably get the most value out of doing one-on-one customer interviews. If you've got an existing product and market and want to figure out how much to charge - maybe A/B testing some different pricing models would be the best approach. What's your context?    Embed Quote
