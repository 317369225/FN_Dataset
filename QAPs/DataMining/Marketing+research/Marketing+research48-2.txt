★Is market research necessary for new product ideas?Apple does not do market research for their products. Japanese "Kaizen" means "continuous improvement", which is in relation to quality but it also recommends launching a product with minimal market research or no market research and then improving the product after launch based on users comments and feedback.
*Disclosure: I am the UK Manager of Marketest a market research company   You should always conduct market research for any product idea. Even if it is similar to products that are already on the market, it is essential to know what customers think of this product, improvements that customers want, and what extra features they desire.   Further to this once your product idea is defined, whether your product is similar to others on the market or unique, you should always testing your product on your target market.    Embed Quote
