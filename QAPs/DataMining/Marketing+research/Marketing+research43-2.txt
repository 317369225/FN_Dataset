★How do market research companies collect and collate reports?
MR companies have primarily use Qualitative and Quantitative research via various methods. The data is collected through questionnaires, focus groups, interviews, surveys and more ways. Finally the data that is collected from different methods are organized in a spreadsheet formats and then analyzed. Today there are a flurry of tools available for doing market research in all different fields, which have their inbuilt data collection and report generation features. So data collection is the easiest task today. Finally you either use the reports the tool provides, or you can extract all the data you want in a tabular format, and create your own reports using a spreadsheet app or upload the data in CollateBox and get some automated reports.    Embed Quote
