★Is market research useful for a small business?Is it worth it?
Market research is an essential part of any business that wants to offer products or services that are focused and well targeted. Customer experience is a valuable information source because it allows you to measure how well you currently meet their expectations and also tell you whether something is right or wrong. Market research can help shape a new product or service, identifying what is needed and make sure that the development of a product is highly focused towards demand. Lastly, research may highlight glaring holes in your service or short falls in your product, regular market research will show if improvements are being made and, if positive, will help motivate a team.
