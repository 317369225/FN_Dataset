★What are the best market research techniques?
The best way is to interview customers. Market research companies are expensive to hire, so that might not be too suitable for startups. As a startup, you want to go through the problem interview, solution interview, and MVP interviews, where at each stage you interview your potential customers to get a better feedback on your idea and product.
