★What is the future of market research?
I WORK IN A MARKET RESEARCH COMPANY Big companies often shell out lot of money in conducting market  research. The information which companies seeks is often about the  competitors strengths and weaknesses and its market position. The market research companies often  do not have resources to collect the data. At least 40 percent of the figures are made up by the analysts. This may go up depending upon the difficulty of projects. Market research companies often adopt in-ethical methods of data collection. Companies are spending on these inaccurate and incomplete data. Now that big companies have analyzed the situation they have started to  set up research wing in the company only.
