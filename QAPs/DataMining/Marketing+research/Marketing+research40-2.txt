★What do marketing research companies do?
Marketing research companies do market survey about all the things required by their clients. They do survey and research about behavior of people in certain situations, the products used by them, how often do they use the product etc. It all depends on what their customer asks them to do. What it eventually does for the customer that it gives them the pattern of product usage and from that, they  formulate their marketing as well as sales strategies.    Embed Quote
