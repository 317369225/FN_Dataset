★Difference between Market Research and Market Analysis?Market research and Market Analysis, both are knowing about the market or understanding the market. What's the major difference between these two?
Market research is doing the research, asking people, doing surveys etc etc - provides the raw data you can then do analysis on. Market analysis is reviewing all that material and Prepare your won view of the market, and subject to what you want from it e.g. Need to know the size of the market/need to know how competiion are etc.    Embed Quote
