★What are some real-world examples of predictive analytics?
The EMBERS project team working out of Virginia Tech has been successful in forecasting civil unrest in Latin America for the past two years, with an average lead time of around 7 days. It uses big data and text analytics to do its forecasting. It's fascinating how it all comes together. Read the story here: http://www.basistech.com/predict... Key events forecast by EMBERS since project inception include protests after the impeachment of Paraguay’s president (May 2012), the “Brazilian Spring” (June 2013), Hantavirus outbreaks in Argentina and Chile (2013), violent protests led by Venezuelan students (Feb 2014), and presidential elections in Panama and Colombia (2014).    Embed Quote
