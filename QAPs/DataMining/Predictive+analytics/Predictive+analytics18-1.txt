★What skills are needed in the field of predictive analytics?
Two parts. One, the definition of the term predictive analytics continues to evolve to include decision making and optimization, beyond the well-known predictive modeling and forecasting. Assuming we are referring to the conventional definition, if you are asking about the skill set and knowledge required to perform projects in the field of predictive analytics, I would refer to the wikipedia page on predictive analytics for a list of mathematical techniques that you need to be well versed in. But to truly thrive in this field, you need to: Have a deep understanding of the domain in which you are operating Be passionate about data Be good at pattern recognition and Have a forensic mindset    Embed Quote
