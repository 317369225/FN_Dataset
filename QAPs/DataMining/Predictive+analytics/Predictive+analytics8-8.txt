★Predictive Analytics: In regression modelling, is more sample data always better?
For regression analysis, large data definitely can take a lot of time and computation for processing. Apart from these aspect, many business need comes with a scenario to work with large data so that the model can capture variation in more precise manner. Though you will need to develop methods of subsampling and working on partial datasets so you can plan your workflow when computing with the entire dataset. The data movement will take much time so you need skills with processing large databases.
