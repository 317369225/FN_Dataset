★How would you make a regular expression match everything but a certain string?
You can construct such a regex recursively.  A string that isn’t “hello” is either the empty string, or a string that begins with a character other than “h”, or the concatenation of “h” with a string that isn’t “ello”.Continue this reasoning until we arrive at the base case: a string that isn’t “” is matched by .+.  The result is 1
