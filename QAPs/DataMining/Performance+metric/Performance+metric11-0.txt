★What are some estimated key performance metrics of companies like Square Space and Wix?
Many startups in the SaaS space focus on customer acquisition and customer churn as main indicators of success. If you can add them on the front end faster than you lose them, you're going to grow. I'm sure there are many additional diagnostic metrics used, but the two mentioned here are potential KEY performance indicators. Edit: Cost per acquisition and customer lifetime value are two more specific metrics that get at acquisition and churn more succinctly. Grow LTV and shrink CPA to maximize growth.    Embed Quote
