★If you run a B2B company, what are the most important performance metrics for your VP of Marketing?
Lead generation: volume, cost and velocity of high quality leads with a consistent program to drive and nurture leads to sales.  Verified by revenue generation. Branding: positive visibility with key influencers and salesforce satisfaction with collateral and sales aids. Driving brand consistency throughout company. Product: Leadership in product definition, positioning and competitive differentiation in defined target market(s) Depending on scope, role could also include alliances, channels, strategy & pricing.    Embed Quote
