★What does a Quora Ontology Architect do?Sounds rather grand and enigmatic at the same time. But what is it? (Layman's terms, please!)
He/she looks at information architectures.  In other words, he looks at how the language used may mean something to some people, and confuse others. Ontology is related to meaning. A Quora Ontology Architect will look at the meaning of the product Quora. Is it in line with what the company wants to share with its user?
