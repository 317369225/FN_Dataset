★What is ontology?
It's a study of what 'is'.  Example questions are 'Is there an external, independent treasury?', 'If a tree falls over in the forest and nothing is around to hear it, has it made a noise?', 'Do people or animals have consciousness or a soul?' Ontology can be contrasted to another major area of philosophy - epistemology, the study of how we know stuff. Often the subjects bleed into each other...    Embed Quote
