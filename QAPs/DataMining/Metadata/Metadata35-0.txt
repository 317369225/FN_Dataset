★Does a Hbase backup actually backup the data stored or just the metadata?Hbase is using HDFS as the underlying storage. HDFS by default stores data in 3 different data nodes, so it is designed to prevent failures.  What does a Hbase backup really do. I would think it is the metadata.Backing up petabytes of data would take a long time.
It does actually do a backup of the data. You can schedule a full backup or incremental backup based on your needs. As you can imagine, its painful to backup petabytes of data. Search for "HBase backup" and you'll find ways large companies optimize their HBase backup strategies.
