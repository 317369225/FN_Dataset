Collective Intelligence: What are best tools and techniques for documenting and sharing "tacit knowledge" within an organization?
It's not just about the tools, it's about the people. There are many people who fear sharing their knowledge because it means they lose value. Management need to be forthcoming with reassurance—letting staff know that by sharing their knowledge they actually increase respect and trust. Credit those who DO share knowledge with recognition and appreciation. But if you do want to talk tools, I know Oracle's InQuira is used by several large and well-known internet businesses.    Embed Quote
