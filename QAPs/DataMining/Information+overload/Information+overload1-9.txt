★What innovations are focusing on dealing with information overload?
Check out http://trendspottr.com. A new real-time search and curation service  that filters, aggregates and publishes the top trending data from Twitter and Facebook for any search query. TrendSpottr's unique algorithms identify the most relevant, timely and trending information from the real-time stream and curates the content into 6 categories: headlines, videos, images, phrases, hashtags and places. The service is built on an API that is available to publishers and third parties that want to augment their editorial and curated content with real-time trending data from the social Web.
