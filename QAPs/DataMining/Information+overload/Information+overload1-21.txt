★What innovations are focusing on dealing with information overload?
Meshin is working on the precept that it is in fact information filter,  but more importantly in context to what you are doing.   Filters or collaboration that is not relevant to what you are trying to do has no meaning.  So now we enter an era where meaning based, or contextual filters are essential.  Couple that with an on the go world that accesses information from many places and you not only have filter issues but limitations on how to get the most important meaning based information in spaces that may have limited real estate.    Embed Quote
