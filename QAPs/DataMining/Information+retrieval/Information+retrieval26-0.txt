★What is the use of ranking algorithms in information retrieval?Give an example where this algorithm can be applied. Why this algorithm is important? (It is one of the question in my exam paper)
Ranking algorithms are used to rank webpages, usually ranking is decided on the number of links to a page. Ranking algorithms are used by search engines of Google, Yahoo. They are used to retrieve webpages provided some keywords. Without these algorithms there was no sure way to tell whether the information presented to us is the best available out there, people would have to scan through many sites to find the required content.    Embed Quote
