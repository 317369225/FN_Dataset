What are the fields where big data analysis is used?
Big Data analysis is revolutionizing almost every field there is. At the core of it, it seeks to help understand and improve systems by measuring a lot of smaller data points as well as a large amount of recurring data. Some common Big Data applications that are already visible are in: Science: meteorology genomics connectomics complex physics simulations biological and environmental research   Business: retail Internet search finance business informatics.
