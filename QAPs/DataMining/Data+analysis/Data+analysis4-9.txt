Which are some of the best Data Analysis tools?
That depend on your business purpose, if you wanna to solve to solve timely calculation and analytics for your realtime decision making with not-too-big data, Excel and esCalc will be enough; if your big data to deeply explore the data value, R, SAS, SPSS would be good choice. There is an article about the comparison of advantages and disadvantages about some of the above tools at the blog, Easy Data,  which may help you to choose the right tools. But I have to mention that the BA must to cooperated well with Both business and technology, the right tools are much better than the latest or advanced tools    Embed Quote
