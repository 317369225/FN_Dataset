What is the difference between "industrial espionage" and "competitive intelligence"?
Industrial espionage is associated with criminal intent and action by a competitors agent or acquisition of confidential information which causes one organization to gain an advantage over others in their industry.  Spying on your competition then using the information to harm or gain an unfair advantage.  Competitive intelligence is the legal and ethical acquisition of information about organizations who compete for the same customers with similar offers.    Embed Quote Updated 21 Dec, 2013. 644 views.
