What are the best books (in English) on competitive intelligence analysis?
There are lots and lots of good competitive intelligence analysis books. For example: Strategic and Competitive Analysis: Methods and Techniques for Analyzing Business by Craig S. Fleisher & Babette Bensoussan. This is a really useful text covering several techniques (and the follow-on book is also worth considering).
