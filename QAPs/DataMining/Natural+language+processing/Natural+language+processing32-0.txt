★What is natural language processing in layman's terms?
Natural Language Processing, in its simplest definition, is the ability for a computer/system to truly understand human language and process it in the same way that a human does. Notable Examples: Apple - iOS 8 - Siri Meet Cortana for Windows Phone Google now One other, basic example: If you were to ask an English speaker "What's up?" they would most likely reply with "Not much", or whatever they're doing. A computer without a sophisticated NLP software would probably try to look up the definition for "up", and reply with:  "up əp/ adverb 1. toward the sky or a higher position.
