★What are the current hot topics in natural language processing?
Natural language processing is currently (late 2013) hot in general. According to Gartner Hype Cycles report, it is nearing the apogee of hype (just before the "trough of disillusionment"). The hottest topics are: by far margin from the rest, natural language understanding and specifically natural language user interfaces. extraction of actionable intelligence from social media or customer feedback. This includes sentiment analysis, but the binary sentiment analysis is no longer enough. named entity extraction. classic "binary" sentiment analysis is already old news.    Embed Quote
